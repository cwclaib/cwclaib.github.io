Alumni Stadium - Chestnut Hill, MA

Alumni Stadium has been used by Boston College since its completion prior to the start of the 1957 season; it replaced Alumni Field.

At the close of the 2022 football season, NC State has a record of 3-6 at this field, all as conference games. It is 2-0 while ranked, and 0-1 while their opponent is ranked.

Alumni Stadium opened in 1957 with 27,000 seats [1]. With the help of alumni John F. Kennedy, the inaugural game was a matchup against the US Naval Academy. In 1971, the stadium's capacity was expanded to 32,000; in 1988, the stadium was renovated to have matching upper decks and a new press box. In 1994, the track around the football field was removed; this move expanded the seating capacity to 44,500 [2]
