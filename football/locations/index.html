Fields Of Play

Below is a list of fields that NC State has played at from its first season in 1892 through 2022. A field's replacement field is only noted in this table if NC State has also played there. "Closed" refers to when professional and/or intercollegiate events stopped taking place at a venue, whereas demo'd (short for demolished) is when the field was torn down, if at all.
