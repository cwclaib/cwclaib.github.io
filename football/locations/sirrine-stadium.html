Sirrine Stadium - Greenville, SC

Sirrine Stadium was used by Furman from partway through the 1936 season until 1980; it replaced Manly Field and was replaced by Paladin Stadium, which NC State has yet to visit.

Overall, NC State had a record of 3-0-2 at this field, going 1-0-2 when the Wolfpack and Hurricanes were Southern Conference mates and winning both non-conferance games on the grounds. One game was canceled in 1952
