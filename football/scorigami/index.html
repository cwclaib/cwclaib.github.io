Scorigami

"Scorigami" is a way of tracking historic sports scores first conceptualized by sports historian Job Bois in his popular video, Every NFL Score Ever | Chart Party. The document is a way to visualize whether a game has ever ended with a specific score, organized by the winning team's score along the x axis and the losing team's score along the y axis. I have adapted the concept to cover NC State's football record specifically, and the results are shown below.

For example, if NC State has ever had any game end 24-17, regardless of winning or losing, that game will be represented below. Mouse over a score to see the most recent instance of the game result. This implementation does not work on mobile; my apologies.
