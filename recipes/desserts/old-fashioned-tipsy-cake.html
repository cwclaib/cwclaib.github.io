Old Fashioned Tipsy Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 dozen plain lady fingers
  • 1/2 lb blanched almonds
  • 1 pint boiled custard
  • 1 pint whipped cream
  • 1/4 cup sweet wine
  • Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Line Pyrex pan with lady fingers, cut in half. Dot with whole blanched almonds.
  2. Pour over the wine. Let stand for a while.
  3. Pour boiled custard over this and add whipped cream to which sugar, to taste, has been added.
  4. Put in refrigerator and chill.

This is an old Southern recipe, used especially at Christmas time.

Source: Lura M. Williams, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
