Mrs. Snypes' Apple Goody - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 3 cups tart apples
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 cups brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup uncooked oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 3/4 cup chopped nuts

Instructions:

  1. Mix apples, white sugar, and lemon juice together and place in a buttered baking dish.
  2. Cream butter and brown sugar and add oatmeal, flour, spices, and chopped nuts. Mixture will be very stiff and crumbly. Cover the apple mixture with this crust mix.
  3. Bake in moderate (350 degree) oven about 40 minutes. Serve with whipped cream or ice cream

Source: Mrs. William B. Grimes, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
