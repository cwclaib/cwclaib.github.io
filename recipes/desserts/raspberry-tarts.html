Raspberry Tarts - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups light brown sugar
  • 1 cup margarine
  • 3 or 4 cups flour (depending on weather and flour)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 50¢ (12 oz?) jar raspberry jam

Instructions:

  1. Cream margarine and sugar. Add eggs and 3 cups flour (use extra portion when rolling dough), with with soda and salt.
  2. Roll out dough 1/2 inch larger than muffin or tart shell pans. Fit into pans which have been greased and coated with flour, leaving some extra space for shrinking.
  3. Mix vanilla with jam and dab 1 tbsp in center of each tart. Top with ground nuts if desired.
  4. Cross with little pieces of dough. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees.
  5. Glaze with creamed butter and pulverized sugar if desired.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
