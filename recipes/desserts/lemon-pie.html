Lemon Pie - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tsp grated lemon rind (or 1/4 tsp lemon extract)
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • one 15-oz can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 4 tbsp sugar

Instructions:

  1. Combine juice and rind and stir slowly into milk. Add egg yolks and stir until blended
  2. Beat white until stiff, adding sugar gradually, and beat stiff for meringue
  3. Bake at 325 degrees until brown - about 15 minutes

Source: Miss Johnnie Banks, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
