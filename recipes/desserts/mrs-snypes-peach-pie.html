Mrs. Snypes' Peach Pie - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • Fresh peaches
  • Pie crust, unbaked
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter

Instructions:

  1. Cut fresh peaches in half, take stones out and place stone side up in unbaked pie shell
  2. Make hard sauce by creaming together 1/2 cup white sugar, 1/2 cup brown sugar, and 1/2 cup butter
  3. Spread hard sauce on top of peach halves and between cracks. Bake slowly until done

Source: Mrs. William B. Grimes, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
