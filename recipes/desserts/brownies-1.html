Brownies (1) - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 squares chocolate
  • 1/2 cup broken nuts
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • a little salt

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter and chocolate over hot water. Add sugar.
  2. Beat in eggs. Add flour, salt and baking powder.
  3. Blend, but do not beat too much.
  4. Add vanilla and nuts.
  5. Bake in 9-inch square pan lined with wax paper in 350 degree oven. While still warm, cut in squares.

Source: Gertrude Hill, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
