Sour Cream Fudge Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 3 egg whites
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1-1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1-1/4 cups cake or pastry flour
  • 4 squares chocolate
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp boiling water
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

  1. To the egg whites, beaten until stiff, add sugar. Beat 5 minutes
  2. Add egg yolks, well beaten. Add 1/2 cup of the sour cream alternately with the flour.
  3. Melt chocolate over hot water and add it to the other 1/2 cup of sour cream. Add this to first mixture.
  4. Dissolve soda in boiling water. Add salt, soda and vanilla to mixture.
  5. Bake in loaf pan or 2 layer pans in moderate oven (350 degrees). Loaf requires about 50 minutes; laers until they leave sides of pan (about 25 minutes).

Source: Mrs. William G. Mordecai, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
