Picnic Caramel Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • Cake
    • 2 cups sifted cake flour
    • 3 tsp baking powder
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 2/3 cup butter or shortening
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 3 egg yolks, well beaten
    • 1/3 cup milk
    • 1 tsp vanilla
    • 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
  • Frosting
    • 3 cups brown sugar
    • 1 cup water
    • 1 tbsp butter
    • 1 tsp vanilla
    • Cream

Instructions:

  1. Cake
    1. Sift flour once more, measure, add baking powder and salt, and sift three times.
    2. Cream butter thoroughly, add sugar gradually, creaming well after each addition.
    3. Add egg yolks; then flour and milk alternately, a small amount at a time. Mix thoroughly after each addition. Add vanilla and fold in egg whites.
    4. Bake in two greased 9-inch layer pans for 25 to 30 minutes in moderate oven (375 F). Double recipe for three 10-inch layers.
    5. Put layers together and cover top and sides of cake with Caramel Frosting.
  2. Frosting
    1. Boil sugar and water until syrup forms a soft ball in cold water (238 F).
    2. Add butter and vanilla, and remove from fire.
    4. When cold beat until thick and creamy.
    5. This with cteam until of right consistency to spread.
