Pandowdy - Desserts

(See also: Bunglet and Apple Pan Dowdy)

Ingredients:

  • some sour, juicy apples (Jazz apples, Granny Smith)
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • lemon peel
  • bread crumbs

Instructions:

  1. Pare, core and slice thin some sour, juicy apples.
  2. Butter a deep baking dish or pan and put in a layer of apples
  3. Sweeten with brown sugar and flavor with lemon peel
  4. Strew (scatter/spread) over a layer of bread crumbs and bits of butter; repeat this until dish is nearly full
  5. Finish with a layer of bread crumbs, bits of butter, and a sprinkle of brown sugar
  6. Bake til apples are soft
