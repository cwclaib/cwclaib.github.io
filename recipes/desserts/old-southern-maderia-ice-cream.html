Old Southern Maderia Ice Cream - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 qt cream
  • 2 qts sweet milk (whole milk)
  • 3 oz Maderia wine (Madeira wine)
  • 1-1/2 oz vanilla extract
  • 3/4 oz essence of pepsin (get at drug store) ***
  • 2-1/2 cups sugar

*** Replace essence of pepsin with rennet

  • Heat 2 quarts (8 cups) of milk to 90F.
  • Dilute 2 tablets or 2 tsp liquid rennet in 4 cups non-chlorinated water.
  • ake 16 tsp of the diluted rennet and add it to the milk.
  • Stir gently for 30 seconds.
  • If the rennet is working, the milk surface will form a slight film in 2 minutes and will have formed a firm curd within 6 minutes.

Instructions:

  1. Warm milk, add cream and sugar (will curdle).
  2. Add wine, extract, pepsin, and freeze

This recipe was passed on to me by my sister-in-law, Mrs. Benton Farmer. It was given to her by her grandfather. It was always his favorite dessert and was passed to him, so she said, by his father and is supposed to have been "concocted" by her great-grandfather.
