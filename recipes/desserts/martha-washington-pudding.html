Martha Washington Pudding - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 2 scant cups bread crumbs
  • Milk to cover crumbs
  • 3 eggs, seperated
  • 1-½ cups sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ lb seedless raisins
  • 1 cup sweet wine

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar and soak bread crumbs in milk
  2. Beat egg yolks thoroughly, then mix with soaked bread crumbs, then add raisins and wine and blend
  3. Pour into buttered baking dish and bake in moderate oven (350 F) until pudding begins to set
  4. Remove and cool, then spread top with meringue made from egg whites and 3 tbsp sugar and ½ tsp vanilla.
  5. Brown in slow oven.
  6. Serve hot or cold

Source: Mrs. R. C. Lanning, Davidson County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
