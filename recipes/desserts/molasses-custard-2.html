Molasses Custard (2) - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup molasses
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp powdered ginger
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2-1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar, then add eggs and mix well
  2. Add molasses.
  3. Sift flour and combine with dry ingredients then sift
  4. Add water to dry mix
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients and bake in moderate oven (350 F) for 45 minutes

Source: Mrs. Walton Aycock, Wayne County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
