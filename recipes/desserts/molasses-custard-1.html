Molasses Custard (1) - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tumbler molasses (1/2 pint)
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp soda

Instructions:

  1. Mix together, adding butter last
  2. Bake in pastry

Source: Mrs. R. S. Ferguson, Alexander County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
