Sand Tarts - Desserts

Note: when I compared this to other recipes for sand tarts online, the results were very different. Nonetheless, this recipe was delicious and a hit with everyone who tried it.

Ingredients:

  • Batter:
    • 1/2 cup shortening
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1 egg
    • 2 cups plain flour
    • 2 tsp baking powder
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1/2 cup nuts
  • Topping:
    • 1 egg white
    • 1 tbsp sugar
    • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients from shortening to nuts together -- will be very thick
  2. Chill and slice very thin.
  3. With a pastry brush, brush each cookie with a mix of second group of ingredients
  4. Top with additional chopped nuts or a pecan half
  5. Bake at 375 for about 10 minutes.

Source: Holiday House Cookbook (1968)
