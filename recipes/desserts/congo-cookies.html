Congo Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 sticks butter or margarine
  • 1 lb light brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3-3/4 cups sifted flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chopped nuts
  • 1 package of chocolate or butterscotch bits

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter, sugar and eggs.
  2. Add dry ingredients, then nuts and bits.
  3. Cook at 350 degrees until done.

Source: Mrs. Everett Briggs, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
