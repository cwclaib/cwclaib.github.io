Pound Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1-3/4 cup sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 cups sifted flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • juice of 1/2 a lemon

Instructions:

  1. Cream 1 cup butter. Add 1-3/4 cup sugar and cream.
  2. Add 5 eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  3. Beat in 2 cups sifted flour, then add 1 tsp vanilla and juice of 1/2 lemon.
  4. Bake in medium-sized stack pan, well greased and floured in 325 degree oven for 1 hour.

Source: Gertrude Hill, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
