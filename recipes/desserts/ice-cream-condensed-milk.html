Ice Cream (Condensed Milk) - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 can condensed milk
  • fresh sweet milk
  • 1 qt fresh fruit

Instructions:

  1. Thin can of condensed milk to proper consistency with fresh milk
  2. Sweeten and flavor to taste
  3. Just before it begins to freeze, add fresh fruit - preferably berries

This recipe came from an old hand compiled cook book. It has been handed down through three generations.

Source: Mrs. L. L. Harvin, Pender County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
