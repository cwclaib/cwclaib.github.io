Rum Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter or margarine
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup raisins
  • rind of 1 orange

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar. Add well-beaten eggs.
  2. Mix dry ingredients and sift together.
  3. Add dry ingredients alternately with buttermilk.
  4. Put raisins and orange rind through a very fine grinder, mix with a little flour and add to batter.
  5. Bake in two 9-inch layer pans at 350 degrees until cake springs back from sides. While layers are still hot, pour rum sauce over each of them, then frost with a rich chocolate icing.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
