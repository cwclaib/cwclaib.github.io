Easy Chocolate Drop Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup margarine (soft)
  • 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
  • 1-1/4 cup sifted flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate bits (or chopped dates or raisins)

Instructions:

  1. Cream margarine. Beat in sugar gradually. Mix in flour thoroughly.
  2. Add vanilla and chocolate bits (or nuts or raisins) and mix well.
  4. Drop by teaspoonfulls on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake about 20 minutes or until light brown at 325 degrees.

Makes 2 dozen cookies. You may leave out vanilla and chocolate bits and add 1/4 cup orange marmalade. This makes a flat cookie; so allow for spreading on pan.

Source: Sylbert Pendleton, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
