Caramel Spice Cake - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • Cake
    • 2-1/4 cups cake flour, sifted
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • 3/4 tsp baking soda
    • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
    • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
    • 1/16 tsp pepper
    • 3/4 cup shortening
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
    • 3 eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla
    • 1 cup buttermilk or 1 cup sour milk
  • Frosting
    • 1/2 cup butter
    • 3 cups powdered sugar, sifted
    • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
    • 4 tbsp cream
    • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Cake
    1. Sift together dry ingredients
    2. Cream together shortening and sugar until fluffy
    3. Add vanilla and then eggs, 1 at a time, beating well after each egg
    4. Add dry ingredients, alternately with buttermilk
    5. Beat 2-3 minutes using high speed of mixer
    6. Pour into 2 greased and floured 8" circular pans
    7. Bake at 350 for 25-35 min (or bundt for 42 minutes) and let cool
  2. Frosting
    1. Melt butter in saucepan, then add brown sugar and cook over low heat 2 minutes, stirring constantly
    2. Add cream and continue stirring until it comes to a boil
    3. Remove from heat and gradutally blend in powdered sugar and vanilla. Add extra cream if needed
