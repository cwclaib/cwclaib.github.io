Maple Candy - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 can sweetened condensed milk (~ 7.5 oz, 2/3 cup)
  • 2 boxes powdered sugar (~ 2 lb)
  • 2 sticks margarine (1 cup)
  • 3 tsp maple flavoring
  • 1 block wax (~ 4 oz, 1/4 lb)
  • 2 small or 1 large packet chocolate chips (~ 12 oz)
  • 1 cup nuts

Instructions:

  1. Combine condensed milk with maple flavoring
  2. Cream margarine and sugar, then add the condensed milk
  3. Add nuts and work in well
  4. Roll mixture in a ball, pat out on aluminum foil, then freeze for 10 minutes.
  5. Cut into squares
  6. Melt wax and chocolate chips in a double boiler, making sure to keep the mixture hot
  7. Use a toothpick to dip squares into hot mixture, then lay squares on aluminum foil to cool

Source: Holiday House Cookebook (1968)
