Crem Brulee - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 quart cream
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 8 egg yolks
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • light brown sugar
  • whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat cream in double boiler until hot but not boiling. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  2. Pour over yolks, well beaten, and add vanilla.
  3. Put in shallow Pyrex baking dish of the right size to have the custard 1-1/2 inches thick. Place in pan of hot water and bake in slow oven until set.
  4. Cool and place in refrigerator to chill thoroughly. Then cover the surface with light brown sugar (dark will not do), 1/4 inch thick.
  5. Place under blazing hot broiler and watch carefully. The top of the sugar should melt leaving a shiny caramel cover.
  6. When entire surface is glazed, remove from oven and cool. Put back in refrigerator and chill thoroughly.
  7. Custard must be served ice cold to be good. You may serve it with whipped cream on top.

Source: Mrs. J. M. Winfree, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
