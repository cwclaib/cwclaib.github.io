Southern Chess Pie - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 tbsp corn meal
  • 2 tbsp crea
  • 4 egg whites, beaten
  • a few grains of salt

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar. Add beaten egg yolks and then corn meal mixed to a paste with the cream.
  2. When well blended, fold in egg whites beaten stiff into which salt has been added.
  3. Pour into rich uncooked pastry shell.
  4. Have oven set at 400 degrees. After 5 minutes reduce heat to 350 degrees and continue baking until silver knife inserted in middle of pie comes out clean.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
