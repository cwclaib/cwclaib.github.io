Pecan Pie - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Karo syrup
  • 1/2 cup pecan meats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs, unbeaten
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 uncooked pie shell

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients and put into pie shell
  2. Bake about 45 minutes, or until custard has thickened

Source: Mrs. John A. Archer, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
