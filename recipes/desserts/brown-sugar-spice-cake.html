Brown Sugar Spice Cake With Baked Icing - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 7/8 cup shortening
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 2-1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp cloves
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sour milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Cream shortning; add sugar gradually and cream thoroughly.
  2. Add whole eggs to 2 egg yolks and beat well; then beat into creamed mixture.
  3. Sift flour, spices, soda, baking powder, and salt together. Add to creamed mixture alternately with sour milk.
  4. Pour into 8-1/2 x 12-1/2 inch pan which has been greased and floured.
  5. Beat remaining egg whites until they hold a point; add brown sugar, sprinkle with nuts, and cover cake with icing.
  6. Bake in 350 degree oven about 45 minutes.

Source: Abbie P. Burch, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
