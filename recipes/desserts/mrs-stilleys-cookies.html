Mrs. Stilley's Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup melted butter or margarine
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1-1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup nuts, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded, dry coconut

Instructions:

  1. Mix melted butter and sugar. Add beaten egg.
  2. Mix dry ingredients and sift together. Add alternately to creamed mixture with milk.
  3. Finally, add raisins, nut, and coconut.
  4. Drop by teaspoonfuls on greased sheet, giving room for cookies to spread.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.

On the first day these cookies are crunchy; thereafter they are chewy.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
