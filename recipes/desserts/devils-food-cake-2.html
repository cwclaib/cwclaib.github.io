Devil's Food Cake (2) - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups brown sugar (packed)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups cake flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup sour milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 squares chocolate
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar. Add whole eggs and beat until light.
  2. Add flour (sifted with salt and soda) and milk and water alternately.
  3. Add melted chocolate. Mix well and pour into greased pan.
  4. Cook at 350 degrees about 20 minutes.

Source: Mrs. Everett Briggs, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
