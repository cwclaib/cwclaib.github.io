Kay's Tombstone Fudge - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 2 squares chocolate
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 tbs vanilla
  • pinch salt

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter on low heat
  2. Stir in milk, bring to boil
  3. Add sugar, vanilla, and salt.
  4. Cook to softball stage (when glob dropped in cool water makes ball shape)
  5. Pour on marble slab, cool, beat, and eat.
