Maple-Pecan Pie - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup maple or brown sugar
  • 1 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1-1/2 cups coarsely chopped pecans
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar.
  2. Add flour and salt, eggs, and syrup.
  3. Add nuts last and mix well.
  4. Bake in unbaked pie shell for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
