Apple Pie - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • Crust
    • 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp kosher salt
    • 1 tbsp sugar
    • 1 cup (2 sticks) very cold unsalted butter, cut into 1/2‚Äù cubes
    • 4 to 8 tbsp ice water
  • Filling
    • 1 paste double crust
    • 2 cups sliced apples
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 3 tbsp water
    • 2 tbsp flour
    • 4 tbsp butter
    • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Crust
    1. Add 1-1/2 cups flour, salt, and sugar to a medium bowl. Stir 2 to 3 times until combined
    2. Scatter butter cubes into flour and mix briefly to coat the butter with flour
    3. Cut the butter into the flour with a pastry cutter or fork, until flour has a coarse, mealy texture similar to fresh bread crumbs.
    4. Add remaining cup of flour. Work butter and flour until flour is evenly distributed
    5. Sprinkle ice water over the mixture. Using a rubber spatula, press the dough into itself. The crumbs should begin to form large clusters. It is ready once you pinch the dough and it holds together
    6. Remove dough from bowl and place in a mound on a clean surface. Work the dough just enough to form a ball. Cut ball in half then form each half into discs. Wrap each disc with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 1 hour, and up to 2 days.
  2. Filling
    1. Prepare bottom crust and fill with tart cooking apples sliced very thin
    2. Sprinkle sugar over them; add water, then distribute butter in small lumps over surface
    3. Sprinkle with flour and then the cinnamon; adjust top crust, well perforated
    4. Bake in moderate oven
    5. If preferred, top crust may be latticed strips of paste

This recipe has been in my family for 30 years.
