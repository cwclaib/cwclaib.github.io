Chip and Cherry Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 3 well beaten eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1-1/2 cups sifted flour
  • 1-1/2 cups baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 lb chocolate bits or chopped semisweet chocolate
  • 2 cups coarsely chopped pecans or walnuts
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped cut dates
  • 1 cup halved candied cherries

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs and sugar together; sift dry ingredients.
  2. Add chocolate, nuts, dates and cherries to flour; then fold in the egg-sugar mixture.
  3. Pour into oiled, wax-paper-lined loaf pan, about 9 x 5 x 3 inches. Bake in 325 degree oven.
  4. After baking 1-1/2 hours to crusty brown, remove and cool on rack. Trim and wrap for Christmas.

Makes 1 loaf.

Source: Mrs. Armistead J. Maupin, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
