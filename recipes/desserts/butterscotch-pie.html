Butterscotch Pie - Baking

Ingredients:

  • Pie
    • 2 cups dark brown sugar
    • 6 tbsp flour
    • 1/8 tsp salt
    • 2 egg yolks
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 2 cups milk
    • 1 premade pie crust
  • Meringue
    • 2 egg whites
    • 6 tbsp sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Filling:
    1. Blend sugar, flour, and salt, then add well beaten egg yolks, butter, and milk
    2. Cook in the top of a double boiler until thick
    3. Cool slightly, then pour into a baked pie crust
  2. Meringue
    1. Beat the egg whites until they will hold their shape, add sugar one spoonful at a time.
    2. Beat until dry; add vanilla and cover pie.
    3. Cover with meringue and bake in a moderate oven until lightly browned

This recipe was brought from Kentucky in 1884 and has been in use by our family since that time.

Source: Mrs. Robert Anderson, Person County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
