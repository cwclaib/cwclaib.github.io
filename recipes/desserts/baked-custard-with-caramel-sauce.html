Baked Custard With Caramel Sauce - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 lb brown sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 quart milk
  • water
  • Nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Use a baking pan large enough to hold a quart of milk.
  2. First, put 1 lb. of brown sugar in the pan on top of stove and caramelize the sugar.
  3. When sugar becomes syrup-like, set it aside to cool. (It will become very hard).
  4. In separate pan heat 6 eggs, 3/4 cup white sugar and 1 quart milk. When hot, pour over brown sugar and set in pan of water.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour. Sprinkle with nutmeg.
  6. Let cool and place in refrigerator. Serve icy cold with whipped cream on top.

Source: Mrs. E. T. Burr, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
