Caramel Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups light brown sugar (packed)
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup nut meats
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • dash salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Break eggs into large bowl and beat lightly; add sugar and beat well.
  2. Add flour that has been sifted with baking powder and salt.
  3. Add vanilla and nuts and beat well.
  4. Divide into two pans (about 9-inch) which have been greased and floured. Bake at 350 degrees for 1/2 hour or until brown on top.
  5. When lukewarm cut into squares.

Each pan makes 16.

Source: Mrs. Elmore V. Barnes, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
