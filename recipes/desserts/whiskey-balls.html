Whiskey Balls - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 3 cups vanilla wafers, crushed
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1-1/2 tbsp cocoa
  • 3 tbsp white syrup
  • 6 tbsp sherry (or more)
  • 1 cup nut meat, cut cinely
  • Powdered sugar

Instructions:

  1. Shape ingredients from vanilla wafters to nut meats into balls.
  2. Roll balls into powdered sugar and place on waxed paper.
  3. Store in refrigerator until ready to use.

This short bread is very much like commercial Lorna Doones. It is rich and so good it melts in your mouth.

Source: Mrs. Jack Wardlaw, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
