Brownie Pie - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp cocoa
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sifted flour
  • 1 stick margarine
  • 1 cup sugar

Instructions:

  1. Melt cocoa and margarine.
  2. Beat eggs and sugar, beating until creamy.
  3. Add the flour, then the chocolate mixture to the egg mixture
  4. Pour into a greased 8” or 9” pyrex pie pan and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Source: Holiday House Cookbook (1968)
