Moonbeam Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 2-1/2 tbsp confectioners sugar (2 tbsp + 1-1/2 tsp)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup sifted cake flour
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla, mixed with 1/2 tsp ice water
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts
  • 1/2 cup confectioners sugar

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and first amount (2-1/5 tbsp) sugar. Add salt, flour, and vanilla (mixed with ice water).
  2. Stir in nuts and shape into balls, walnut size.
  3. Finally, flatten to 1/2-inch thickness. Bake 30 minutes at 300 degrees, until lightly brown.
  4. While still hot, shake cookies in paper bag with 1/2 cup confectioners sugar.

Makes 20

Source: Mrs. Leslie R. Ames, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
