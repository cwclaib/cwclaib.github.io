Chocolate Refrigerator Pie - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups crushed vanilla wafters
  • 1 cup chopped nuts
  • 1/2 cup or 1 stick melted butter
  • 1 pkg. semisweet chocolate drops
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 6 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Mix crumbs, nuts and butter. press 2/3 of this into bottom of pie plate and chill.
  2. Melt chocolate with water in top of double boiler. Cool.
  3. Whip cream and fold chocolate and sugar into cream. Add vanilla.
  4. Pour into plate and sprinkle remaining crumbs over top.
  5. Chill 12 hours.

Source: Mrs. A. R. Tucker, Jr., Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
