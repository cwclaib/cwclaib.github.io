Mom's Pecan Chess Pie - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • 1 stick melted butter/margarine
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup pecans (chopped)
  • pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs slightly with fork
  2. Add sugar and flour; stir until dissolved
  3. Add milk, margarine, and vanilla and mix well
  4. Stir in pecans
  5. Pour in pie crust and cover edge with foil
  6. Bake at 350 oven for 30 minutes, then remove foil and cook for 20 minutes
