Pecan Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1/3 lb butter
  • 1 lb sugar
  • 1 scant cup flour
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 lb pecan meats
  • 1-1/3 lb raisins
  • 2 whole nutmegs
  • 1 large wine glass of brandy or sherry

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs, flour, and brandy.
  3. Lastly add nut meats.
  4. Bake slowly (about 250 degrees) in loaf pan for 4 hours.

Source: Mrs. William G. Mordecai, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
