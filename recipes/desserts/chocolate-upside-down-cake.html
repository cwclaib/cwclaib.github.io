Chocloate Upside Down Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3-1/2 tbsp cocoa
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 2 tbsp cocoa

Instructions:

  1. Sift together flour, sugar, 1-1/2 tbsp cocoa, and salt, then add milk, butter, and vanilla
  2. Pour batter into greased 8 x 8 inch pan.
  3. Make syrup with sugars, hot water, and 2 tbsp cocoa. Boil 5 minutes and pour over top of batter.
  4. Bake 40 minutes at 350 degrees.

Source: Alma S. Young, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
