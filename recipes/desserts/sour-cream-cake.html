Sour Cream Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup sour milk or cream
  • 3 tbsp cocoa
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Cream sugar and butter with cinnamon and cocoa.
  2. Sift soda with flour and beat in alternately with sour cream.
  3. Grease loaf pan and line with greased paper. Cook at 350 degrees for 50 minutes.

See also: Sour Cream Cake Icing

Source: Mrs. William A. Howell, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
