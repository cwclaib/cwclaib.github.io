Rosy Cinnamon Apples - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cinnamon candies
  • 2-3/4 cups boiling water
  • 8 small apples, peeled and cored
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve candies in boiling water and then add apples. Cook slowly until tender, turning frequently. Remove apples to shallow dish.
  2. Add sugar to syrup and boil 3 minutes. Pour over apples, turning carefully to glaze each.
  3. Cook.

Serve as dessert or as garnish for meat. Or stuff with mixture of cream and cheese and nuts and serve as salad. Serves 6 to 8.

Source: Florence H. Jones, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
