Brownies (2) - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 4 squares chocolate, divided
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1-1/2 tsp vanilla, divided
  • 1-1/2 tbs shortening
  • 1-1/2 cups confectioner sugar
  • 3-1/2 tbsp milk

Instructions:

  1. Cream together 1/2 cup butter, 1 cup sugar.
  2. Add 2 eggs. Beat thoroughly.
  3. Sift 3/4 cup flour. Add and sift together 1/4 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp baking powder.
  4. Add sifted ingredients to creamed mixture. Add 2 squares melted chocolate, 1 cup walnuts and 1 tsp vanilla.
  5. Pour into greased pan and bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. Frost with brownie frosting:
    1. Melt 2 squares chocolate and 1-1/2 tbsp shortening.
    2. Measure 1-1/2 cups sifted confectioner sugar and add pinch salt, 3-1/2 tbsp milk and 1/2 tsp vanilla.
    3. Blend. Add hot chocolate mixture.

Source: Gertrude Hill, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
