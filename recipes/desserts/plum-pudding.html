Plum Pudding - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb butter or suet
  • 1 lb grated bread crumbs
  • 1 lb brown sugar
  • 10 eggs, seperated
  • 1 lb currants
  • 2 lb raisins
  • 1 lb citron
  • 1 lemon, rind and juice
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp allspice
  • 1-1/2 tsp cloves
  • 1-1/2 tsp mace
  • 1/2 tsp soda
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup red wine and lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Mix spices in 1/4 cup wine and lemon juice
  2. Mix bread crumbs with fruit
  3. Beat eggs separately
  4. Mix all ingredients (beaten eggs whites last) and bake for 4-to-5 hours.

Source: Mrs. John G. Wood, Chowan County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
