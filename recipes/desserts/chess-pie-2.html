Chess Pie (2) - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 9 egg yolks
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sweet milk
  • 2 tbsp flour (rounded)

Instructions:

  1. Beat yolks thoroughly. Add mixture of sugar and flour. Beat.
  2. Add melted butter and milk. Pour into uncooked pie crust.
  3. Bake 10 minutes at 450 degrees. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake until firm (about 50 additional minutes)

Makes 1 large chess pie or 2 small ones. If I cut this recipe in half, I use 5 egg yolks and bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes and about 20 additional minutes at 350 degrees. If the entire recipe is made into 2 pies instead of 1 large pie, use cooking time for half recipe.

Source: Mrs. William Daniel, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
