Molasses Drop Cookies - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1/2 cup sour milk
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cloves (allspice)
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2-1/2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup chopped raisins

Instructions:

  1. Cream shortening and sugar
  2. Dissolve baking soda in sour milk, mix with molasses and add them to the cream
  3. Sift salt, cloves, cinnamon, and ginger with flour.
  4. Combine the dry ingredients with the wet and add the egg (well-beaten) and raisins
  5. Using teaspoons, drop onto greased pan about 2 inches apart
  6. Bake in moderate oven at 375 for 7-10 minutes

Source: McDowell County Extension Homemakers papers (unfortunately, I failed to note exactly which document it came from)
