Slight Pudding - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 3 pints milk
  • 5 heaping tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • lemon
  • 6 eggs, separated
  • teacup of sugar (just under 3/4 cup)

Instructions:

  1. Thicken over heat (medium?) 3 pints of milk with 5 heaping tbsp flour and 1 tbsp butter
  2. While this is cooling, beat the yolks of 6 eggs with a teacup of sugar
  3. Beat the whites to a stiff froth and stir in lightly
  4. Bake a light brown in a slow oven, flavor with lemon

This recipe is much over 100 years old, being used in the Forney family for generations.
