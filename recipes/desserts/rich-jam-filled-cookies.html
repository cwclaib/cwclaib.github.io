Rich Jam-Filled Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 sticks butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • jam or jelly of your choice
  • About 3 dozen almonds

Instructions:

  1. Cream 1-1/2 sticks butter and 3/4 cup sugar. Add 2 egg yolks.
  2. Add 2 cups of flour, sifted with 1/2 tsp salt. Add 1/2 tsp vanilla.
  3. Roll into balls about 1" in diameter. Do not chill.
  4. Dip sewing thimble in flour and make indenture in middle of each ball. Fill hole with jam or jelly (strawberry, grape, mint or other).
  5. Slant 1 almond on side of jam-filled hole.
  6. Cook at 375 degrees on greased cookie sheet.

Makes about 3 dozen small cookies. Do not crowd; cookies spread.

Source: Elizabeth C. Harrelson, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
