Chiffon Crown Cake - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • Cake:
    • 3/4 cup boiling water
    • 1/2 cup cocoa
    • 1-2/3 cup flour
    • 1 + 3/4 cup sugar
    • 4 tsp baking powder
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/2 cup cooking oil
    • 7 eggs, seperated
    • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Icing:
    • 1/2 cup margarine
    • 3 cups confectioners sugar
    • 5 tsp instant coffee
    • 4 tsp cocoa
    • 4 tsp light cream
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Chiffon Crown Cake
    1. Gradually add boiling water to cocoa and stir until smooth and cool.
    2. Sift into a mixing bowl the flour, 1 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    3. Make a well in the dry mixes, then add in order the cooking oil and 7 egg yolks. Beat mixture until smooth, adding in the cocoa mixture and add 1 tsp vanilla.
    4. Combine separately from other mix 7 egg whites and 3/4 cup sugar and beat until stiff
    5. Pour egg yolk mix gradually over whipped egg whites, gently folding until just blended.
    6. Turn into ungreased 10" tube pan and bake at 325 for 55 minutes, then set oven at 350 for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Icing
    1. Cream margarine with confectioners sugar. Beat until smooth.
    2. Add instant coffee, cocoa, light cream, and vanilla. Mix together well.
    3. Cover top and sides of cake with icing, then decorate with chopped nuts if desired

Source: Holiday House Cookbook (1968)
