Moravian Christmas Tree Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 pint molasses (Porta Rican and New Orleans)
  • 1/2 lb brown sugar
  • 1/2 lb butter or margarine
  • 1 tbsp ground allspice
  • 1 tbsp ground cloves
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp baking soda, dissolved in water
  • 1 quart flour (more if needed)

Instructions:

  1. Melt molasses, sugar and butter in bowl over hot water or in warm oven. Stir well.
  2. Pour in flour and spices and add soda. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let stand 24 hours, then roll thin as paper and cut into shapes.
  4. Bake in moderately hot oven, 450 degrees, until brown.

Source: Florence Jones, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
