Chocloate Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • Mixture No. 1
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1/2 cup butter or other shortening
    • Pinch salt
    • 2 beaten egg yolks
    • 3/4 cup light corn syrup
  • Mixture No. 2
    • 1-1/2 cups flour
    • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 egg whites, beaten lightly

Instructions:

  1. Mixture No. 1:
    1. Cream sugar with butter or other shortening.
    2. Add salt, egg yolks, and corn syrup.
  2. Mixture No. 2:
    1. Combine ingedients
  3. To mixture No. 1 add alternately mixture No. 2 with 3/4 cup milk.
  4. Stir in 2 lightly beaten egg whites.
  5. Pour into 8 x 8 x 2 inch greased pan. Bake until done, about 45 minutes, in 300 degree oven.

Source: Mabel Kamphoefner, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
