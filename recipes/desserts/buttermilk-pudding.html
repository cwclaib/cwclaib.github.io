Buttermilk Pudding - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 large tbsp butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • 1 small tsp of lemon flavored soda

Instructions:

  1. Bake in dish in moderate oven until set but not curdled.

Source: Mrs. R. S. Ferguson, Alexander County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
