Black Walnut Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dates
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 6 tbsp flour
  • pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix together all ingredients but the egg whites.
  2. Stiffly beat egg whites, then add.
  3. Pour into small muffin pans and bake.

Makes 18 small cakes.

Source: Mrs. Sam Ruffin, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
