Molasses-Walnut Pie - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp flour, blended with
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup black walnuts, chopped coarsely

Instructions:

  1. Mix ingredients together in order given. Add nuts last.
  2. Pour into unbaked pastry shell and bake 35 to 40 minutes in 350 degree oven.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
