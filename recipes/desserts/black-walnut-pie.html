Black Walnut Pie - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • butter size of a walnut
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs. Add sugar, butter, milk, and walnuts.
  2. Lastly add vanilla.
  3. Bake in a plain unbaked pie crust at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
