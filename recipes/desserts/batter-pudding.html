Batter Pudding - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • Pudding
    • 1 qt sweet milk
    • 4 eggs
    • 4 tbsp flour
  • Hard Sauce
    • 1 stick softened butter
    • 1-1/2 cups powdered sugar
    • 2 tbsp whiskey

Instructions:

  1. Pudding
    1. Mix ingredients.
    2. Bake in cool oven (about 270) until set, but do not overcook
  2. Sauce
    1. Beat butter until fluffy
    2. Add powdered sugar gradually, then add whiskey and beat again
  3. Serve with hot hard sauce

Recipe is over 100 years old. It was the favorite of Mrs. J. R. Johnston who lived at Zuela near Lucia in Gaston County, grandmother of Mrs. Ferguson.

Sources:

  • Sauce: The Pioneer Woman - Best Hard Sauce Recipe
  • Pudding: Mrs. R. S. Ferguson, Gaston County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
