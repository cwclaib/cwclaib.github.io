Apple Crumb Pudding - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 6 large tart apples
  • 1/2 to 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 tbsp shortening
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 cup sifted flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Cream

Instructions:

  1. Cut apples into eighths. Cook about 10 minutes with sugar, cinnamon and water.
  2. Place in deep, greased 9-inch pie pan or shallow baking dish.
  3. Cream shortening; add brown sugar and cream well.
  4. Sift flour, salt and baking powder and add to shortening, working in with hands.
  5. Sprinkle over apples and bake about 45 minutes at 350 degrees. Serve with cream, hot or cold

Source: Ruth Firth, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
