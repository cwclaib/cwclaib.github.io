Nana's Banana Pudding - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1 small (3.4 oz) box of vanilla pudding mix - NOT INSTANT PUDDING - plus whatever ingredients it calls for
  • About 6 bananas - very ripe, not green (they're sweeter)
  • A box of Nilla wafers
  • 1 cup (additional) of milk
  • 1 (additional) lightly beaten egg
  • 1/2 cup (additional) sugar
  • 1 tbsp (additional) butter

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the standard banana pudding mix as instructed on the box, except to the mix, add:
    1. 1 cup of milk
    2. 1 lightly beaten egg
    3. 1/2 cup sugar
    4. 1 tbsp butter
  2. Cook pudding mix until it thickens
  3. Then layer in serving bowl. Wafers, banana slices, pudding. Wafers, banana slices, pudding. Try to end up with a last layer of wafers and pudding.
