Merry Company Cake - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 2-3/4 cup Swan's Down cake flour
  • 2 tsp Calumet baking pwder
  • 1-1/2 tsp salt
  • 1-3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup shortening (2 sticks)
  • 3/4 tsp orange extract
  • 3/4 tsp almond extract
  • milk: 1/2 cup with vegetable shortening; 3/4 cup with butter or margarine
  • 3 eggs and 1 egg yolk, unbeaten

Instructions:

  1. Have shortening at room temperature. Preheat oven at 375 degrees. Grease and lightly flour 9-inch tune pan.
  2. Stir shortening in bowl until just softened.
  3. Sift flour once before measuring. Measure dry ingredients into sifter and sift into shortening.
  4. Add milk to flavoring and mix with flour mixture until all flour is dampened.
  5. Beat two minutes at medium speed in mixer.
  6. Add eggs and yolk and beat 1 minute in mixer. Scrape mixer bowl often.
  7. Turn batter into pan and bake in moderate oven (375 degrees) for 1 hour or until done.
  8. Cool cake in pan 15 minutes. Loosen from sides with spatula or knife and turn right side up on rack to cool.

Source: Mrs. Ruth F. Will, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
