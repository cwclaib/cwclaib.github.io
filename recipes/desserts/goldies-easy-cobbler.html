Goldie's Easy Cobbler - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 6 tbsp butter or margarine
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 can peaches, blackberries, or any other fruit

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Put butter in deep baking dish and set in oven to melt
  3. Make a batter of sugar, flour, baking powder, salt and milk. Pour over melted butter. Do not stir
  4. Put fruit on top; do not stir.
  5. Bake for 1 hour

Source: Collard Greens, Watermelons and "Miss" Charlotte's Pie (1993, 1996)
