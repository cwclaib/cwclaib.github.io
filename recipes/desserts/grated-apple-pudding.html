Grated Apple Pudding - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 4 large apples, grated
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 2 oz butter
  • 2 eggs
  • Nutmeg or allspice, ground

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients. Season with nutmeg or allspice
  2. Bake slowly (300 degrees) until apples are very done.

Source: Mrs. J. M. Winfree, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
