Peanut-Butter Balls - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup margarine, melted
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 3-1/2 cups graham crackers, crushed
  • 1 can coconut (~ 3.5 oz)
  • 1 box confectioners sugar (~ 1 lb)
  • 1 12-oz package semi-sweet chocolate morsels
  • 1/4 block paraffin wax

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter, stir in peanut butter, graham cracker crumbs, coconut, and sugar.
  2. Roll out into small balls and dip in the secondary mixture
  3. Melt chocolate morsels and wax
  4. Dip balls into melted chocolate mixture

Source: Holiday House Cookbook 1986
