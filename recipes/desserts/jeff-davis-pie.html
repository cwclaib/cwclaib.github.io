Jeff Davis Pie - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup plum or damson preserves
  • pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Beat egg yolks, add sugar and then flour
  2. Then mix butter, milk, and vanilla flavoring
  3. Roll out crust and put in pie tin, spread the preserves along the bottom and pour the custard mixture overtop of them
  4. Bake in a slow oven (300 deg); when done, cover with meringue and brown

This recipe is very old.

Source: J. A. Harris, Sr., Montgomery County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
