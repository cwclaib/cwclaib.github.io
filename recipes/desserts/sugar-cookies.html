Sugar Cookies - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 2-¼ cups flour
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • ½ tsp. nutmeg
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • grated rind of 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp. milk

Instructions:

  1. Mix ingredients and roll into thin sheet on a slightly floured cloth on the board.
  2. Cut with cookie butter and sprinkle with sugar.
  3. Bake in oven hot (400) for 10-15 minutes

Source: I failed to note where exactly this recipe came from, however it was from somewhere in the McDowell County Extension Homemakers papers; I don't believe it was the same Holiday House Cookbook that many of the other recipes came from.
