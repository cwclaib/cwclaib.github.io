Honey Apple Crisp - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 4 cups sliced apples
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup almonds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread sliced apples in a shallow baking dish. Sprinkle with sugar and lemon juice, and pour honey over all.
  2. Mix in a bowl the flour, brown sugar and salt, and work in the butter, making a crumbly mixture.
  3. Spread these crumbs evenly over the apples.
  4. Bake in a moderate oven (375 degrees) for 30 to 40 minutes or until apples are tender and crusp is crisply browned. Serve warm with plain or whipped cream topped with a dash of cinnamon.

Source: Nancy Baker, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
