Silver Dollar Cookies - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Pecan halves

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar. Add beaten egg yolk and then flour. Add vanilla
  2. Roll in small balls the size of a silver dollar. Flatten with fork and place pecan half on top of each cookie.
  3. Bake in hot oven (400 degrees) until lightly brown.

Source: Alma S. Young, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
