Cow Cookies - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup nuts or raisins
  • 1 package chocolate chips, if desired (~ 1 lb)

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in order and drop scant teaspoon on cookie sheet
  2. Bake 12 minutes at 350

Source: Holiday House Cookbook (1968)
