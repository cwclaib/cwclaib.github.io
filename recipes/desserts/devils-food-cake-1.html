Devil's Food Cake (1) - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Swan's Down cake flour
  • 1-1/2 cups sugar
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 squares baker's chocolate
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • dash salt

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter; add sugar gradually.
  2. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well. Then add melted chocolate.
  3. Sift flour before measuring; then sift with soda, salt, and baking powder, 3 times.
  4. Beat in flour mixture and milk, adding alternately.
  5. Mix well and bake in two 9-inch layer pans at 375 degrees for 30 mins.

Source: Mrs. Elmore V. Barnes, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
