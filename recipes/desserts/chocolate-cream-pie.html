Chocolate Cream Pie - Desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar + 4 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 cups scalded milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs, separated + 2 eggs
  • 2 squares chocolate
  • 1 tsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Melt the chocolate in the milk
  2. Mix dry ingredients together, then add milk and chocolate gradually
  3. Cook 15 minutes in double boiler, stirring constantly until mixture thickens, and afterwards occasionally
  4. Mix a small amount of the mixture with the egg yolks and return to the double boiler; cook about 3 minutes
  5. Cool and add vanilla
  6. Fill baked pie shell and cover with meringue made from two egg whites and four tbsp sugar

Source: Eileen Jordan, Wilkes County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
