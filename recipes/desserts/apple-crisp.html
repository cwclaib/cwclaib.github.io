Apple Crisp - Dessert


Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 heaping tbsp flour
  • 1-1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped apples
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts
  • 1 cup whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Beat egg well; ad sugar, beating until light and creamy.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder and salt together and add to egg mixture. Blend well.
  3. Fold in apples and nuts. Add vanilla.
  4. Pour into greased-paper-lined dish. Bake in slow oven (325 degrees) for 30 minutes. Serve with whipped cream.

Source: Mrs. J. M. Winfree, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
