Good Cheese Spread - Appetizers


Ingredients:

  • 1 3-oz pkg. cream cheese
  • 3 tsp finely chopped olives
  • 1/2 tsp anchovy paste
  • 3/4 tsp angostura bitters
  • 1 tsp finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 tsp prepared mustard (or dry)

Instructions:

  1. Mix and serve on crackers.

Source: Elizabeth C. Harrellson, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
