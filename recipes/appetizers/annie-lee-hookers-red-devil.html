Annie Lee Hokoer's Red Devil - Appetizers


Ingredients:

  • 1 lb American cheese, diced
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • salt and red pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Put cheese and soup in double boiler and heat until cheese is melted and sauce smooth.
  2. Add seasonings.
  3. Serve hot on toast or crackers.

Source: Mrs. William G. Mordecai, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
