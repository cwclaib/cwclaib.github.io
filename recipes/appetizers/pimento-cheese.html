Pimento Cheese - Appetizers

("Pimento Cheese Sandwich Spread")

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb American or mild cheese
  • 1 small (4 oz) jar pimento
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. When cheese is softened to room temperature, grate or mash until creamy.
  2. Pour pimento with liquid from jar and mash with fork until fine.
  3. Combine cheese and pimento, add mayonnaise, salt, and pepper to taste.
  4. Cream or mix until fluffy.

Source: Mrs. Jim Bell, Cabarrus County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
