Pigs In A Blanket - Appetizers

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Pillsbury crescent rolls
  • little smokies hot dogs
  • seasonings (garlic salt, sandwich spread, etc)

Instructions:

  1. Break a few eggs into a bowl and mix them up so the whites and yolks are together
  2. Open the crescent roll can and spread out the crescents; cut each crescent into thirds
  3. Dip each crescent piece in the egg wash, then roll up a hot dog inside each one
  4. Sprinkle seasonings on top
  5. Bake 12-15 minutes at 375
