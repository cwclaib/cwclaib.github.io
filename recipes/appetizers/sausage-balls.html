Sausage Balls - Appetizers

Ingredients:

  • 3 scant cups of Bisquick baking mix
  • 1 lb pork sausage at room temperature
  • 16 oz sharp cheddar cheese, shredded (4 cups)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives or parsley (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a stand mixer at mix at low speed until all ingredients are evenly combined. The dough is stiff, so if mixing by hand use a wooden spoon or your hands to work ingredients into a dough
  2. Roll dough into 1 Â½ inch balls and place on baking sheet 1 inch apart
  3. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes
