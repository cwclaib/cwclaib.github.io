Adrian's Delight - Appetizers

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz cream cheese
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 envelope unflavored gelatin
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1/2 cup white raisins
  • 1 cup slivered almonds, toasted
  • Grated rind of 2 lemons
  • Saltine crackers
  • clumps of seedless grapes with confectioners sugar

Instructions:

  1. Let cream cheese, butter, and sour cream come to room temperature; cream well, then add sugar.
  2. Soften gelatin in cold water, then stir over hot water in double boiler til dissolved. Add to cream cheese mixture.
  3. Add raisins, toasted almonds, and lemon rind.
  4. Pour into 1-qt mold sprayed with vegetable cooking spray and refrigerate overnight
  5. Unmold and serve spread with saltines (from original: DO NOT substitute saltines -- this really changes the recipe)
  6. Garnish with clumps of sugared, seedless grapes

Makes 1 quart

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
