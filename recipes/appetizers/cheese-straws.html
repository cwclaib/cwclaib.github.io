Cheese Straws - Appetizers


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup cheese
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and red pepper

Instructions:

  1. Mix ingredients thoroughly and roll.
  2. Cut into strips 1/4 inch wide and 4 to 5 inches long.
  3. Bake at 400 degrees until light brown.

Source: Mrs. Harold V. Joslin, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
