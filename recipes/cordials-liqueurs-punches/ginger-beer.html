Ginger Beer - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz ginger
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 lemon cut in slices
  • 1 lb loaf sugar
  • 1 gal. boiling water
  • 2 tbsp yeast (fresh yeast)

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients except the yeast and let stand until it gets cold.
  2. Then add yeast and let it remain all night
  3. Strain it off and bottle it
