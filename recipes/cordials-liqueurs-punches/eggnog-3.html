Christmas Eggnog - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 7 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of sugar (1/2 and 1/4, separated)
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 pint blended whiskey
  • 3 oz dark rum (Jamaican)
  • Grated nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Beat separately yolks and whites of eggs.
  2. Add 1/2 cup sugar to the yolks while beating, and add 1/4 cup sugar to the whites after theyâ€™ve been beaten very stiff.
  3. Fold the egg whites into the yolks, then fold in cream and milk.
  4. Slowly add whiskey and rum.
  5. Stir thoroughly and serve cold with dash of nutmeg on top of each cup.

Serves 10.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (2017)
