Longwood House Milk - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

(Courtesy of Longwood House)

Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon milk
  • 1 gallon vanilla ice cream
  • 2 fifths bourbon (1 handle)
  • 1 fifth rum, medium dark
  • 1 cup creme de cacao
  • 1 quart vanilla ice cream
  • Nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Mix first 5 items one day prior to serving and refrigerate.
  2. At serving time, place the remaining ice cream in punch bowl.
  3. Pour punch mixture over ice cream and sprinkle with freshly grated nutmeg.

Serves 6-8

This lovely white frame house with its rolling lawn serves as the home of the President of Longwood College in Farmville. It was built in 1815 and is the site of the birthplace of Confederate General Joseph E. Johnston. Having once been a tea room where students, faculty and townspeople could meet, it now serves as the scene of many college functions. Thus its charm and beauty have been shared by many young ladies during their scholarly pursuits at this traditional Virginia school.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (1975, 2017)
