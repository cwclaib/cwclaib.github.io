Creme de Mure - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 5 cups blackberries*, washed and de-stemmed
  • 1 bottle of red wine, medium bodied (standard size, 750 mL)
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup vodka/gin

*~78 blackberries, or just over 2 pint containers
(1 cup = 15-16 blackberries, each container holds 30-32 blackberries)

Instructions:

  1. Put the blackberries in a large glass or ceramic bowl and pour over red wine. Then mash the berries using a fork or potato masher to release the berries
  2. Cover with a clean towel and leave to macerate for 48 hours in a cool place (fridge) - give the berries a little mash every now and then if you remember!
  3. Strain through a wire mesh sieve to remove the berries, then strain again through cheese cloth to ensure all seeds etc are out.
  4. Pour into a large saucepan and add the sugar (1-½ cups). Bring to a gentle simmer on a medium heat, stirring until the sugar is dissolved. Simmer for 8 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vodka or gin (½ cup), and then leave to cool completely
  6. Pour into clean, preferably sterilized, bottles and store in cool, dark place. It can be enjoyed immediately, but will keep almost indefinitely if in sterilized bottles - if not, then about a year.

Source: A Tipsy Giraffe - Blackberry Liqueur (Crème de Mûre) Recipe
