Eggnog - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

("Egg Nog")

Ingredients:

  • 6 eggs, separated
  • 1/2 gallon French vanilla ice cream, softened
  • 1 quart of milk
  • 1 cup of bourbon
  • 1/2 cup rum
  • 1/2 cup apple jack brandy

Instructions:

  1. Beat egg yolks, add ice cream and milk.
  2. Slowly add bourbon, rum and brandy.
  3. Beat egg whites and fold in carefully.
  4. Serve with nutmeg.

Serves 10.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (2017)
