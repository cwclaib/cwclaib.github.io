Applejack - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

See also: Hard Apple Cider

Ingredients:

  • Hard Cider
  • Plastic container (1 gallon jug)
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Honey

Instructions:

  1. Place hard cider in mostly full plastic container and freeze for 2+ days, until frozen
  2. Remove from freezer and stab 4-5 times with a butter knife to create passageways for the Applejack to run through
  3. Tip the jug upside down over a bowl to catch the Applejack. It should take 15-30 minutes to finish dripping
  4. Discard ice
  5. Wait for applejack to reach room temp, then add white/brown sugar and/or honey to taste
