Frozen Whiskey Sour - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches


Ingredients:

  • 6 oz frozen orange juice concentrate
  • 6 oz frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 1 cup bourbon
  • A fifth of sauterne
  • 2 fifths champagne

Instructions:

  1. Fill blender with all ingredients and ice and blend til smooth.

Serves 12.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (2017)
