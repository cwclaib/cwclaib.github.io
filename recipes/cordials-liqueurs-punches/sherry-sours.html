Sherry Sours - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • A fifth dry sherry
  • 6 oz frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Mix in blender, store for 3-4 days.
  2. Serve with cherries or strawberries to garnish.

Serves 10-12.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (1975, 2017)
