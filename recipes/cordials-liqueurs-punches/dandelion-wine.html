Dandelion Wine - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon blossoms
  • 1 gallon boiling water
  • 3-1/2 lbs sugar, halved
  • 1 orangs, sliced and de-seeded
  • 1 lemon, sliced and de-seeded
  • 1 cake of yeast (1 packet, 2-1/4 tsp)

Instructions:

  1. Separate the flowers from the petals -- note that any green stems will make the mix sour.
  2. Combine the water and the blossoms and let stand 24 hours and strain.
  3. Add 3-1/2 lbs sugar, 1 orange, lemon, and yeast.
  4. Let stand 4 days.
  5. Remove blossoms and let stand 15 days in warm place.
  6. Strain and let stand 2 days in cool place to settle.
  7. Bottle and cover loose for a day, then tighten.
