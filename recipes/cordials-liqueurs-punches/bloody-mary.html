Bloody Mary - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

("Bloody Mary's For Brunch")

Ingredients:

  • 46 oz tomato juice
  • 1.5 cups vodka
  • 1/4 cup worcestershire
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp tabasco
  • 1/2 tsp cracked pepper
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine ingredients and serve over ice.
  2. Garnish with lemon or lime squeezed over each drink.

Serves 12

Source: Virginia Hospitality (1975, 2017)
