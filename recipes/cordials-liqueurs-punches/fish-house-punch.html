Fish House Punch - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches


Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups of sugar (12 oz)
  • 2 quarts of water (64 oz)
  • 1 quart lemon juice (32 oz)
  • 2 quarts Jamaican rum (64 oz)
  • 1 quart cognac (32 oz)
  • 4 oz peach brandy

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve sugar in water; add all other ingredients.
  2. Allow ingredients to mellow for 2 hours, occasionally stirring.
  3. Pour over ice in punch bowl.

Serves 30.
