Eggnog (2) - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches


Ingredients:

  • 9 eggs, separated
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1-1/2 cups brandy
  • 1 cup whiskey
  • 1/2 cup rum
  • 3 qts. whipped cream (after whipping)

Instructions:

  1. Beat yellows of eggs in electric mixer for 10 minutes, adding sugar.
  2. Pour liquors over yellows and let stand for several hours.
  3. Stir in well-beaten whites and cream

Source: Mrs. Harold V. Joslin, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
