Aged Eggnog - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1 lb sugar
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 pint half and half
  • 1 pint whole milk
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 1 cup Jamaican rum
  • 1 cup cognac
  • 1 cup bourbon
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt

Instructions:

  1. Separate eggs; beat yolks with sugar (1 lb) and nutmeg (1 tsp) until mixture lightens in color and falls off the whisk in a solid ribbon.
  2. Combine dairy, booze, and salt in a second bowl, then slowly beat into egg mixture
  3. Move to a glass jar and store in fridge for 2+ weeks
  4. Serve in mugs or cups topped with extra nutmeg

Source: Alton Brown - Aged Eggnog
