Spiked Cranberry Punch - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 1 quart of cranberry juice
  • 18 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup bourbon
  • 1 cup rum
  • 2 quarts ginger ale, chilled

Instructions:

  1. Combine fruit slices and liqueurs in punch bowl, chill.
  2. Pour in ginger ale just before serving.
  3. Freeze cubes of ginger ale.

Serves 15-20.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (2017)
