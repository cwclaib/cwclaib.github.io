Oleo Saccharum - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 cups (packed) citrus peels - roughly 8 medium lemons
  • 2/3 cup cane sugar
  • 2 tbsp boiling water
  • (optional) 1 tbsp high proof vodka

Instructions:

  1. Wash citrus well; Peel away citrus into a bowl, leaving behind white just beneath the skin (white has bitterness which you want to avoid)
  2. Add granulated cane sugar and stir into bowl to fully coat the peels
  3. Let the mixture sit for at least 6 hours or as long as overnight. Stir a few times if possible
  4. Strain the liquid into a clean container, retaining sugar/peel combination
  5. Pour about 2 tbsp boiling water into peels, stir, and strain again

Yields about 2/3 cup. Stores in fridge for about 2 weeks

Source: Drinking Hobby - How to Make delicious citrus syrup, Oleo Saccharum
