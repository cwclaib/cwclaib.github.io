Irish Coffee - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

("Quick and Easy Irish Coffee")

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 cups coffee
  • 6-8 oz bourbon
  • 1/2 pint heavy cream, whipped and sweetened

Instructions:

  1. Pour bourbon into decanter.
  2. Put cream in silver bowl.
  3. Pour coffee into cups â…” full or less, add 1 oz bourbon and top with a couple dollops of cream - do not stir.

Serves 6-8

Source: Virginia Hospitality (2017)
