Golden Champagne Punch - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches


Ingredients:

  • 46 oz pineapple juice
  • 6 oz frozen orange juice from concentrate
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • A fifth of sauterne
  • 2 fifths of champagne

Instructions:

  1. Mix juices and sugar; chill.
  2. Pout juice mixture over block of ice in punch bowl and add wine

Serves 15-18.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (1975, 2017)
