Almond Wine - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches


Ingredients:

  • 6 lbs seeded raisins
  • 6 lbs sugar
  • 1/4 lb almonds, crushed, not blanched
  • 3 gals water
  • 3 cakes yeast (3 envelopes active dry yeast)

Instructions:

  1. Stir every other day for 30 days.
  2. Siphon and bottle

This makes a delicious sherry wine

Source: Mrs. Harold V. Joslin, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
