Rose Punch - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 3 fifths ros√© wine
  • 6 oz frozen pink lemonade
  • 46 oz fruit punch
  • 1 quart club soda
  • Ice Mold:
    • 1 orange
    • 1 lemon
    • 1 bunch green grapes

Instructions:

  1. Ice Mold
    1. Slice one orange and one lemon in round slices.
    2. Put in 8 inch round cake pan.
    3. Fill pan with water and top with grapes.
    4. Freeze til ready to use.
  2. Combine wine, lemonade and punch.
  3. Add 2 quarts of water.
  4. Just before adding, add ice mold and soda.

Serves 25
