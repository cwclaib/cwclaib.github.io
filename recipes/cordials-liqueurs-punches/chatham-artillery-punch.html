Chatham Artillery Punch - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 2 quarts Maraschino cherries (32 oz)
  • 1-1/2 gallons Catawba wine (192 oz)
  • 1-1/2 gallons strong tea (192 oz)
  • 2-1/2 lbs brown sugar (40 oz)
  • 1-1/2 quarts orange juice (48 oz)
  • 1-1/2 quarts lemon juice (48 oz)
  • 1/2 gallon rum (64 oz)
  • 1 quart gin (32 oz)
  • 1/2 pint Benedictine (16 oz)
  • 1-1/2 quarts rye whiskey (48 oz)
  • 6 pints champagne (96 oz)

Dividing by six gets you:

  • 6 oz Maraschino cherries (~< 1 cup)
  • 32 oz (1 pint, 1.25 bottles) wine
  • 32 oz (1 pint) strong tea
  • 6-2/3 oz (~< 1/2 lb) brown sugar
  • 8 oz orange juice
  • 8 oz lemon juice
  • 10-2/3 oz (~1/2 a fifth) rum
  • 5-1/2 oz (~1/4 a fifth) gin
  • 2-2/3 oz Benedictine
  • 8 oz rye whiskey
  • 1 bottle (750 mL) champagne

Instructions:

  1. Mix 36-48 hours before serving. Add champagne when ready to serve

Source: From a The Pirate House in Savannah, GA
