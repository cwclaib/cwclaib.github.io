Golden Champagne Punch - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches


Ingredients:

  • A fifth sparkling burgundy
  • A fifth champagne
  • Orange liqueur (Cointreau) to taste
  • strawberries

Instructions:

  1. Mix burgundy and champagne, add orange liqueur to taste.
  2. Garnish with strawberries.

Serves 12.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (2017)
