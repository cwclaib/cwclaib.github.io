Rum Punch - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 4 fifths of rum
  • 2 quarts pineapple juice, unsweetened
  • 2 fifths sauterne
  • 2 quarts ginger ale
  • 1 oz bitters

Instructions:

  1. Place ingredients in a large punch bowl.
  2. Garnish with pineapple rings or cut citrus fruits if desired.
  3. Hint, Make ice in a 9 inch ring mold with strawberries or cherries added.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (2017)
