Vodka Punch - Cordials, Liqueurs, & Punches

Ingredients:

  • 1 fifth of vodka
  • 1 quart ginger ale
  • 46 oz apple juice
  • 3 oz concentrated lemon juice
  • Orange slices

Instructions:

  1. Mix together all ingredients, add orange slices frozen in ice block and serve.
  2. May be prepared 1 day ahead of time.

Serves 15.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (2017)
