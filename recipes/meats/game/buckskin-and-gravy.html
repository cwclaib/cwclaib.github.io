Buckskin and Gravy - Game/Other

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb venison or antelope steak, cut 1/2” thick
  • 4 slices bacon
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup Coors beer

Instructions:

  1. Pound venison til 1/4” thick
  2. Cook bacon til crisp and then remove from drippings; reserve the drippings
  3. Cook onion in drippings til tender and set them aside
  4. Combine flour, salt, and pepper; coat meat with flour mix
  5. Slowly brown meat on both sides in drippings til done, about 10 minutes
  6. Remove meat; blend remaining flour from meat coating into drippings, then stir in milk and Coors. Cook and stir til thickened, and then 1-2 minutes more.
  7. Season to taste
  8. Spoon gravy over meat then sprinkle crumbled bacon on top

Makes 4 servings

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
