Venison Cutlets - Game/Other

Ingredients:

  • Tender cuts from loin
  • oil
  • grape juice
  • salt
  • pepper
  • flour
  • eggs, slightly beaten
  • bread crumbs
  • butter
  • currant jelly

Instructions:

  1. Take cuts from loin about 1/2”- to 3/4”-thick and marinate in oil and grape juice for 1 hour
  2. Drain a sprinkle with salt and pepper, roll in flour, dip in slightly beaten egg and roll in fine bread crumbs
  3. Saute in butter 10 to 12 minutes, turning frequently
  4. Place meat on hot platter; add ¼ cup currant jelly to drippings, stir to a boil and pour over steak.
  5. Allow 1/2 to 3/4 pound per person

From Foods and Nutrition Department, Home Demonstration Division of the Extension Service

Source: From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
