Black "Mammey's" Baked Ham - Pork

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium sized cured ham
  • 1 quart sweet cider
  • 1 tbsp whole cloves
  • 1 tbsp (level) dry mustard
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baclk pepper
  • 1 cup cracker crumbs, blended

Instructions:

  1. Saw off shank and enough of butt end to leave a nice shaped piece. Skin with a sharp knife if it is not one of the “Skinned” hams, and soak overnight in cold water.
  2. When ready to cook, wipe dry with cloth and rub well with the mustard, and place in roaster.
  3. Tie cloves in a muslin bag and put into cider and heat to boiling point
  4. Pour over ham and put into oven and bake, allowing about 20 minutes to the pound or until tender and, basting often with cider.
  5. When done, remove to shallow pan and rub into fat the sugar, pepper and cracker crumbs, then return to oven to brown.

Source: Mrs. E. F. House, Sampson County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
