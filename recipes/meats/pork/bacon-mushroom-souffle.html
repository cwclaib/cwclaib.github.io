Bacon-Mushroom Souffle - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 3 strips bacon, cut into 1" pieces
  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/4 lb American cheese
  • 4 well-beaten egg yokls
  • 4 stiff-beaten egg whites

Instructions:

  1. Fry bacon; remove and drain.
  2. Add flour to bacon drippings; blend.
  3. Add soup and cheese; stir until smooth.
  4. Gradually add sauce to egg yolks, then carefully fold in egg whites, beaten stiff but not dry.
  5. Pour into ungreased 1-1/2 qt. casserole. Place in a shallow pan with 1" water surrounding it. Bake at 350 for about 55 minutes.

Serves 6.

Source: Mrs. Alexander Badger, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
