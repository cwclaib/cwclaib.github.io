Country Style Pork Chops - Pork

Ingredients:

  • 6 pork chops
  • 2 tbsp fat
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups cold water

Instructions:

  1. Roll chops in flour to which salt and pepper have been added
  2. Put fat in pan when hot and brown chops quickly on both sides. Remove from pan.
  3. Add one tbsp flour to hot grease, brown and add Â½ tsp salt and the 2 cups of cold water and stir until smooth.
  4. Add chops to gravy and cook in oven for one hour at 325.

This recipe was used by my Grandmother Doub, who was Caroline Spach of the Friedberg Moravian group. She was of the Spach family of the Rock House, which is being rebuilt.

Source: Mrs. W. R. Lasley, Forsyth County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
