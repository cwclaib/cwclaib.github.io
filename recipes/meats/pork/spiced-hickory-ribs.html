Spiced Hickory Ribs - Pork

Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1-1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 4 lbs pork loin baby back ribs or spareribs
  • 1/2 cup peach preserves
  • 1/2 cup Coors

Instructions:

  1. Combine ginger, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper and rub onto the meaty side of the ribs. Cover and chill for 2 hours
  2. About an hour before cooking time, soak hickory chips in water to cover
  3. Lace ribs accordion style on spit rod; secure with holding forks
  4. Arrange hot coals on both sides of shallow foil drip pan, drain the hickory chips and sprinkle some on top of coals
  5. Attach spit; position drip pan under meat
  6. Turn on rotisserie and lower hood
  7. Grill ribs over medium coals for 1 hour or til done
  8. Sprinkle coals with chips every 20 minutes
  9. In blender, combine peach preserves and Coors and blend til smooth. Brush mixture over ribs during last 10 minutes of grilling. Heat and pass remaining glaze.

Makes 4 servings

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
