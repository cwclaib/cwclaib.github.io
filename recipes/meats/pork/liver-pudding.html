Liver Pudding - Pork

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs liver
  • Salt water
  • 1/2 cup cornmeal
  • Sage to taste
  • pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil liver in salt water until tender, then grind in chopper
  2. Mix in cornmeal, sage, and pepper
  3. Place in oven and brown
    1. Before browning, pudding may be stuffed like sausage, stored in ice box or freezer and sliced and fried when needed.

This recipe belonged to Mary Vinson Sasser and is approximately 100 years old. It has been handed down through four generations. Mrs. Saser came from Ireland in a boat when she was five years of age. Two of her brothers fought in the battle at Wilmington during the Civil War.

Source: Wilson County Council - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
