Pork Tenderloin - Pork

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • Jelly

Instructions:

  1. Salt and pepper meat, then pan sear
  2. Using a pan with a rack, line pan with foil
  3. Chop rosemary
  4. Microwave jelly for 30 seconds and rub on pork, then sprinkle on chopped rosemary
  5. Cook at 350 for ~30 minutes, should be light pink on outside and 145 inside
