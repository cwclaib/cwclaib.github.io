Wilbur's Barbecued Pork - Pork

Ingredients:

  • 3 lb pork shoulder
  • Salt
  • Apple Cider vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Run salt lightly over pork shoulder, then baste lightly with vinegar
  2. Place in roasting pan with an inch of water in the bottom
  3. Cover with lid or foil and cook for 5 - 5-Â½ hours in the oven at 450. Meat is done once bone is loose
  4. Remove from pan and shred/chop

Note: to get more authentic flavor, place the meat in a 500 degree oven for 10 minutes exactly. Then reduce to 275 and bake for 5 hours, turning shoulder once each hour.
