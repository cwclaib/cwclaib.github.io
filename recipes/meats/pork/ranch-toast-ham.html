Ranch Toast Ham - Pork

Ingredients:

  • 1 fully-cooked ham center slice, cut 1‚Äù thick (about 2.5 lbs)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 1"-thick bread slices
  • shortening or cooking oil for shallow frying
  • maple-flavored syrup or molasses

Instructions:

  1. In a large heavy skillet cook ham slice in the 2 tbsp of hot cooking oil over medium heat for 16-18 minutes total, turning meat occasionally
  2. Transfer meat to platter and keep warm
  3. In mixing bowl, stir together flour, sugar, and baking soda. Add buttermilk and eggs; beat with smith rotary beater until assimilated
  4. Dip bread into batter -- let stand for 1 minute on each side.
  5. Fry bread in 8-inch skillet in 1/4 inch hot shortening about 1 minute per side or until crisp and golden brown
  6. Serve batter fried bread with slices of ham; pass syrup to drizzle over.

Makes 6 servings

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
