Oven Bar-B-Q - Pork

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup tomato ketchup
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1-1/2 cups water
  • 3 large onions
  • 3-1/2 or 4 lbs beef or pork

Instructions:

  1. Slice meat thin, place in roasting pan
  2. Slice onions over meat
  3. Mix all other ingredients together and pour over the meat
  4. Cook with lid in oven for 2-1/2 hours at 350

Source: Holiday House Cookbook (1968)
