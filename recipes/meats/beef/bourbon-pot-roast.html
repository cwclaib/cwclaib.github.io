Bourbon Pot Roast - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 4 lbs boneless pot roast
  • 4-6 slices bacon, cut into 1/4‚Äù pieces
  • pinch oregano
  • pinch basil
  • pinch garlic
  • pinch pepper
  • pinch salt
  • 1 cup bourbon

Instructions:

  1. Using a sharp knife, cut deep slits all over meat.
  2. Combine bacon and spices in small bowl and mix well
  3. Push bacon into slits in meat
  4. Place meat in large baking pan and pour bourbon over top
  5. Baked, covered, for 3 hours at 325.

Serves 6-8

Source: Foods That Rate At NC State (1983)
