Lasagna - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 cup onion, chopped
  • 1 (28 oz) can tomatoes
  • 1 (12 oz) can tomato paste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano leaves
  • 1 (8 oz) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb Ricotta cheese
  • 8 oz Mozzarella cheese, shredded or thinly sliced
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. In a skillet, brown beef and onions, drain. Add tomatoes,cut them up, and reserve the liquid.
  3. Add paste, then measure liquid from the tomatoes; add enough water to make 2 cups.
  4. Add parsley, sugar, garlic powder, pepper and oregano. Simmer uncovered, stirring occasionally, for 30 min.
  5. Cook lasagna noodles as directed on package; drain.
  6. In 13x9x2 baking pan, spread about 1 cup sauce, then alternate layers of lasagna, sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan. Bake 40 to 50 minutes until lightly browned and bubbly. HINT: you may want to pout 1 eight-oz can of tomato sauce over lasagna before baking
  7. Allow to stand 15 minutes, then cut into squares.

Serves 8

(Note: may be cooked longer at a lower temperature)

Source: Collard Greens, Watermelons and "Miss" Charlotte's Pie (1993, 1996)
