Beef Jerky - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef round steak

Instructions:

  1. Remove fat from steak
  2. Partially freeze steak, cut into very thin strips cutting across grain for crisp jerky and with grain for chewy jerky
  3. Layer strips in bowl and sprinkle with salt and pepper
  4. Rearrange layers and repeat
  5. Weight down with plate and cover. Chill
  6. Drain, pat dry, and arrange on rack in shallow baking pan
  7. Bake at 300 in oven for 45 minutes or til dry
  8. Cool, store in airtight container. Makes 4 oz

Makes 4 servings

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
