Baked Liver - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs calf's liver in one thick piece
  • 4 slices fat salt pork
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 medium sized onion, sliced
  • 1/6 tsp pepper
  • 2/3 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup stewed tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Lay pork strips in slits cut in liver.
  2. Place onion in baking dish, place lover on it, sprinkle with salt, pepper and flour mixture, and pour tomatoes over.
  3. Bake in oven at 350 until tender.

Source: Mrs. Nan K. Sloop, Rowan County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
