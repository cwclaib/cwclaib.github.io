Baked Fillets In Sour Cream - Beef


Ingredients:

  • Lemons, sliced
  • Onions, sliced
  • Steaks
  • Salt
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • Paprika

Instructions:

  1. Cover bottom of shallow baking dish with thin slices of lemon or onion (both may be used).
  2. Place thick steaks or fillets of fish on top. Sprinkle with 1/8 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp pepper.
  3. Cover and bake for 30 minutes at 400 degrees.
  4. Spread lightly with 2/3 cup sour cream and salt. Sprinkle with paprika.
  5. Place low under broiler until lightly browned.

Source: [NOT CREDITED], Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
