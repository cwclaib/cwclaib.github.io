Jimmy V's Pot Roast - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 4-1/2 lbs beef brisket
  • 4 carrots
  • 3 ribs of celery
  • 3 onions, thickly sliced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika
  • 4 cubes beef bouillon
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 cup ketchup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400. Place roast in oven in uncovered pan and cook for 30 minutes or until brown.
  2. Add carrots, celery, onions, bay leaf, salt, pepper, and paprika. Dissolve beef bouillon cubes in water and add to roast with vegetables.
  3. Cover and cook for 2-Â½ hours at 350, or until tender. Slice meat and put back into gravy to serve.

Source: Jim Valvano's Guide to Great Eating (1984)
