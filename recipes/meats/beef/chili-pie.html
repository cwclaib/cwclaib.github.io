Chili Pie Cornmeal Dumplings - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 lbs beef stew meat, cut into 3/4‚Äù cubes
  • 1 cup chopped onion (3 medium onions)
  • 15-1/2 oz can red kidney beans, drained
  • 12 oz can Coors
  • 8-oz tomatoes, cut up
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 4 oz can chopped green chilies
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 3/4 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1/3 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Place meat, onion, and kidney beans in bottom of ungreased 3 qt casserole dish
  2. In bowl, combine Coors, undrained tomatoes, tomato paste, undrained chilies, the garlic, chili powder, 1 tsp salt, and ground cumin and pour over the meat.
  3. Bake, covered, at 350 for 2 hours
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder and 1/4 tsp salt.
  5. Stir in oil, egg and milk.
  6. Using a tablespoon, drop mixture into hot stew, forming 8 dumplings.
  7. Bake covered for 30 more minutes or til dumplings are done.

Makes 8 servings

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
