Pot Roast In Foil - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 4 lbs chuck roast
  • 1 pkg. dehydrated onion soup
  • 1 box fresh mushrooms, 6 or 7 oz. (optional)
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Cut piece of heavy duty aluminum foil large enough to wrap and seal roast. Dot center of foil with half the butter.
  2. Pour on half the dry soup mixture and half the mushrooms, which have been sliced. Place roast on top of this.
  3. Dot top of roast with remaining butter, soup and mushrooms. Seal roast in foil.
  4. Lay in pan and bake at 325 for 3 hours or until tender. When the roast is done, it will be in gravy

This is good served with boiled noodles.

Source: "E" Lynch, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
