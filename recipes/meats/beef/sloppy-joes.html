  • Salisbury Steak - Beef

    • Ingredients:

    • 1-1/2 lb lean ground beef
    • 16 oz package of pork sausage
    • 1 small onion, chopped
    • 1/2 medium sized green bell pepper, chopped
    • 8-oz can of tomato sauce
    • 1/2 cup of water
    • 1/2 cup of ketchup
    • 1/4 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
    • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
    • 2 tbsp yellow mustard
    • 1 tbsp chilli powder
    • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 cup all purpose flour (slow cooker only)
    • 8 hamburger buns, toasted

    Instructions:

    1. Start:
      1. Chop up onion and pepper
      2. Brown beef and sausage with onion and bell pepper in a large Dutch oven at medium-high heat, stirring for 10 minutes or until meat is no longer pink.
      3. Drain well
    2. Slow Cooker Method (see 3 for stovetop)
      1. Place beef mixture in a 4-1/2 qt slow cooker
      2. Stir in tomato sauce and add in tomato sauce, water, ketchup, brown sugar, cider vinegar, yellow mustard, chilli powder, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and flour
      3. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours
    3. Stovetop Method
      1. Stir in tomato sauce and add in tomato sauce, water, ketchup, brown sugar, cider vinegar, yellow mustard, chilli powder, Worcestershire sauce, and salt
      2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat
      3. Cover, reduce heat to medium, and simmer, stirring occasionally (30 min.)
