Beef Tongue - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 1 beef tongue
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp allspice
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • Flour
  • 1/2 cup seeded raisins
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 lemon slice
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Parboil beef tongue in water to cover with salt, pepper, allspice, and gninger.
  2. Remove half the water in which tongue was parboiled; dust the tongue in flour and replace in stewpan; Add raisins and cook for about 1 hour over low heat.
  3. Just before it is done, add brown sugar, lemon slice, and vinegar.
  4. Remove tongue and skin; lightly thicken stock and serve over tongue.

Source: Mrs. Walter Stearns, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
