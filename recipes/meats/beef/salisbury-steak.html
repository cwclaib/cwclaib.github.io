  • Salisbury Steak - Beef

    • Ingredients:

    • 1 lb ground round beef
    • 1/2 tsp black pepper
    • 1 tsp of salt
    • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp of Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 whole egg
    • 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 1 large onion, sliced
    • 2 tbsp flour
    • 2 cups of water
    • 2 tsp beef bouillon granules

    Instructions:

    1. In a mixing bowl, combine the beef, black pepper, salt, garlic powder, Worcestershire sauce, bread crumbs, and egg. Stir well
    2. Shape into 4 equal sized patties
    3. Heat a skillet over medium heat for about a minute, then add butter
    4. Place meat patties in skillet and brown on both sides for about 4-5 minutes each. Remove from skillet and set aside.
    5. Add the sliced onion to the skillet, right on top of the browned bits
    6. Reduce the heat and cook the onion on medium low heat until the onions turn translucent and brown. Don’t rush this step, cook low and slow for about 20-25 minutes
    7. Sprinkle 2 tbsp of flour over the onions, stir. Let cook and brown for 2 minutes
    8. Add 2 cups of warm water, stirring well. Raise the heat back to about medium.
    9. Add 2 tsp of beef bouillon granules. Stir well.
    10. Place the meat patties back in the pan. Cover, reduce heat just a little bit and let simmer for 15 more minutes

    NOTE: Can substitute beef broth for the granules, just don’t add water
