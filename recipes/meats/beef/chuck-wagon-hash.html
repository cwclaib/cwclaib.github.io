Chuck Wagon Hash - Beef

Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 cups finely chopped or cooked ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp dry mustard
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne
  • 2 cups cubed cooked potatoes (2 medium)

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook beef, onion, green pepper and garlic in butter til onion is tender but not brown
  2. Stir in salt, mustard, and cayenne, then stir in potatoes
  3. Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally

Makes 4 servings

Source: A Taste Of The West From Coors (1981)
