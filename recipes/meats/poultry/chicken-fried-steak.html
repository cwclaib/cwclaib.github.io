Chicken Fried Steak - Poultry

Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 lbs beef top round steak cut, 1/2” thick
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 cup Coors beer
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup Coors beer

Instructions:

  1. Cut meat into 6 portions and pound the steak between two pieces of wax paper til 1/4” thick
  2. Stir together the ¾ cup of flour, the salt (½ tsp) and pepper (⅛ tsp).
  3. Dip each piece of meat into flour mixture, then into ¼ cup of Coors, and then back into the flour.
  4. In a 12-inch skillet, brown meat in hot oil, turning once (about 3-4 minutes per side)
  5. Remove meat to platter; keep warm.
  6. Blend the 2 tbsp flour into drippings in skillet, then stir in milk and ½ cup Coors. Cook and stir til thickened and bubbly.
  7. Cook and stir 1-2 minutes more
  8. Pass sauce with meat

Serves 6

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
