Presbytery Chicken - Poultry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup of butter of margarine, melted
  • 2-3 tbsp of dijon mustard
  • 1 (16-oz) package of herb stuffing mix
  • 3 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Mix mayo (1 cup), margarine (Â½ cup) and mustard (2-3 tbsp)
  3. Put herb stuffing mix in blender and process into fine crumbs
  4. Dip chicken breasts in mayo mixture and roll in herb stuffing mix
  5. Place chicken in shallow, ungreased baking dish, cover with foil and bake for 1 hour
    1. Remove foil for last 10 minutes to brown

Yields 8-10 servings

Source: Virginia Hospitality (1975, 2017)
