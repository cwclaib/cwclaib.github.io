Mother's Delicious Chicken - Poultry

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium size chicken, bone in
  • 1 pint can tomatoes
  • 4 onions, chopped fine
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 dozen pieces of toast

Instructions:

  1. Cut up chicken as for stewing and put in stew pan, cover with boiling water and cook slowly until tender
  2. Take pieces of chicken out of liquid and cut meat off bones
  3. Put the bones back in liquid and boil for 30 minutes more.
  4. Remove the bones and put the small pieces of chicken into the liquid, then add tomatoes, onions, salt, pepper, sugar, and butter and cook slowly until the onions are cooked.
  5. Have toast ready to pour over toast; serve hot.

Source: Mrs. E. F. House, Sampson County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
