Alabama Chicken Corn Pie - Poultry

Ingredients:

  • 1 small fryer or boiler
  • 6 ears fresh corn
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Proceed with pastry as for other pies using a top crust.
  2. Prepare chicken and corn as for frying and stew chicken in small amount of water until tender
  3. Melt butter in chicken broth, add salt, sugar, and pepper
  4. Put chicken and corn in layers in large skillet or casserole
  5. Pour broth over chicken and corn.
  6. Bake at 350 for about 25 minutes.

This is a favorite of the mother of Mr. J. W. Roberts of Barbour Co., Ala. He served in the Calvary [sic] during the War Between The States and returned to his home "The Old Roberts Plantation" to live to a ripe old age.

Source: Mrs. Hazel J. Bell, Cabarrus County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
