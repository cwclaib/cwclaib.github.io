Chicken a la Vanderbilt - Poultry

Ingredients:

  • 1 Bermuda onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 8 thin slices cooked ham
  • 8 slices cooked chicken breast
  • 1 cup cream (or less)
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cook onion in butter for 5 minutes
  3. Add mushrooms and saute
  4. Spread onion and mushrooms in a 9x13 baking dish
  5. Add ham. Top each piece of ham with a slice of chicken and enough cream to cover (not a bath, just a dusting)
    1. Try dipping the chicken and ham in the cream and dusting in pre-blended mix
  6. Sprinkle parmesan and paprika on top
  7. Place in oven until brown on top, about 15 minutes

Yields 8 servings.

Source: Virginia Hospitality (1975, 2017)
