Alabama Pie - Poultry

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 4 carrots
  • 2 medium onions
  • couple pieces of celery
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1-1/4 hens

Instructions:

  1. Cook hens and cut up after removing bones and fat.
  2. Cube potatoes and carrots. Chop onions and celery and cook until tender.
  3. Put a layer of chicken and a layer of vegetables; make gravy from stock, season to taste and pour over chicken and vegetables that have been dotted with butter.
  4. Cover with pastry and bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes.

Source: [UNCREDITED], Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
