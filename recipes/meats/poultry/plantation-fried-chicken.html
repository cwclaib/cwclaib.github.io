Plantation Fried Chicken - Poultry

Ingredients:

  • One 2-1/2 lb fryer chicken, cut up
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2/3 cup cornmeal

Instructions:

  1. Dress chicken, remove skin, and place in bowl, then add milk
  2. Place in refrigerator for 3 hours
  3. Blend 1 tbsp of this milk with the egg, then dip chicken in the mixture
  4. Combine the salt and cornmeal, then dip the wetted chicken in this mixture
