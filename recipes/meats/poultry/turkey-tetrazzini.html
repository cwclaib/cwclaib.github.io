Turkey Tetrazzini - Poultry


Ingredients:

  • 1 large pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 4 oz spaghetti
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups cooked, chopped turkey or chicken
  • 1 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • small can of mushrooms, with liquid
  • Cheese
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Chop and brown in frying pan 1 large pepper and 1 onion.
  2. Boil 8 4 oz spaghetti in large pot of water with 1 clove of garlic and 1/2 tsp salt.
  3. Mix 3 cups cooked, chopped turkey or chicken with 1 cup of cream of mushroom soup, and small can of mushrooms (including liquid from can of mushrooms). Add cooked onions and pepper and boiled spaghetti, drained. Salt and pepper as needed.
  4. Place in greased baking dish and cover top with grated cheese. Bake in oven at 375 until brown and bubbly (1 hour)

The original owner of this cook book scaled down the recipe from 8 ozs of spaghetti to 4, but made no mention of whether the rest of the ingredients should be similarly proportioned. The time estimate to cook in parenthesis comes from their notes as well.

Source: Gertrude Hill, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
