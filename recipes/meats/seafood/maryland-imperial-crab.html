Maryland Imperial Crab - Seafood


Ingredients:

  • 1 pt. fresh crab meat (1 lb)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp cracker crumbs
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup sweet cream
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk, beaten
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/2 tsp dry mustard
  • dash hot sauce

Instructions:

  1. Heat cream in double boiler. Add 1/2 butter; when hot, stir in beaten egg yolk. Cook until slightly thickened.
  2. Add crab meat, lemon juice, and seasoning; then fold in the beaten whole egg.
  3. Put in shells or casserole. Cream the remaining butter and stir in cracker crumbs; sprinkle on top of crab mixture.
  4. Bake in moderate oven (350) about 20 minutes.

A bit of green pepper may be added to the crab if desired.

Source: Abbie P. Burch, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
