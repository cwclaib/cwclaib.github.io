Tuna Casserole - Seafood

Ingredients:

  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 green pepper
  • 4 pieces celery
  • 1 stick butter
  • two 8 oz packages of noodles
  • 5 cans tuna
  • Salt
  • Tabasco
  • Black pepper
  • 3 cans mushroom soup
  • Milk or cream
  • Buttered crumbs

Instructions:

  1. Saute 1 chopped onion, 1/2 green pepper, 4 pieces of celery in 1 stick of butter.
  2. Boil two 8 oz packages of noodles until tender, drain and rinse.
  3. Mix with above ingredients.
  4. Scald 5 cans tuna, flake into noodles, season with salt, Tabasco, black pepper and stir in 3 cans mushroom soup.
  5. Add a little milk or cream if it is dry. Top with buttered crumbs.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes.

This recipe, when doubled, makes 2 large pans and serves about 40.

Source: [UNCREDITED], Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
