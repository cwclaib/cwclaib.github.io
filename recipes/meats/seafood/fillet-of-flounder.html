Fillet of Flounder - Seafood


Ingredients:

  • Flounder fillets
  • 2 cups thin cream
  • 1/2 cup sherry
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Brown buttered bread crumbs

Instructions:

  1. Trim fillets and lay side by side in frying pan. Cover with 2 cups thin cream, 1/2 cup sherry, salt and pepper. Poach over low heat from 6 to 10 minutes.
  2. Remove fillets to glass baking dish. Cook cream and sherry until thick, and then pour it over fish.
  3. Run platter under broiler until sauce bubbles and browns in spots. Sprinkle brown buttered crumbs on top and serve.

Source: Mary Hull Hill, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
