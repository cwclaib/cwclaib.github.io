Shad - Seafood

Ingredients:

  • 6 lb shad
  • 2 lbs butter
  • 10 eggs
  • 2 qts toasted bread crumbs
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions:

  1. Place fish in large pan and cover with water. Put in oven and let simmer for 4-6 hours
  2. Combine butter, eggs, bread crumbs, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl
  3. Stuff shad with stuffing and sew shut with needle and thread

Recipe is from 1780 from Lincoln County
