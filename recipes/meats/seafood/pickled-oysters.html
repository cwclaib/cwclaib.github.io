Pickled Oysters - Seafood


Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon oysters, with juice
  • Salt
  • 3/4 cup vinegar (per cup of cooked oyster juice)
  • 1 oz whole cloves (per cup of cooked oyster juice)
  • 1 oz whole black pepper (per cup of cooked oyster juice)
  • 1 oz whole mace (per cup of cooked oyster juice)

Instructions:

  1. Cook 1 gallon of oysters with juice and a little salt until gills curl. Lift oysters and strain out juice.
  2. To every cup of juice, add 3/4 cup vinegar, 1 oz whole cloves, 1 oz whole black pepper, and 1 oz whole mace. Let this cook until thoroughly flavored and then cool.
  3. When cold, add oysters. Put in refrigerator in jars.

Makes wonderful appetizers or cocktails and will keep indefinitely if on ice.

Source: Mrs. W. P. Duff, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
