Scalloped Oysters - Seafood

Ingredients:

  • 1 qt standard-type oysters
  • 2 cups top milk
  • 1/2 lb salty crackers
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup celery
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Add alternating letters of oysters, crackers, and celery in baking dish, beginning and ending with crackers
  2. Dot with butter and black pepper.
  3. Add salt to milk and pour over mixture.
  4. Place baking dish uncovered in oven and back 45 minutes at 345.

Serves six

Source: Mrs. Brown Murdock, Iredell County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
