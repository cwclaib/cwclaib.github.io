The Lazy Southern's Shrimp and Grits - Seafood

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz cooked Andouille sausage
  • 12 oz large, uncooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tsp seasoning salt (optional; I leave this out because it’s salty enough as is)
  • 1 tsp bacon salt
  • 1 box instant cheese grits (twelve 1-oz packets)
  • 3 cups boiling water

Instructions:

  1. In a medium skillet, cook the andouille sausage over medium high heat until hot.
  2. Remove from skillet and slice into coins. Set Aside.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together the grits and boiling water. Sprinkle in bacon salt if using and stir well. Let sit to thicken.
  4. Toss peeled shrimp into the skillet. Sprinkle with the seasoning salt.
  5. Stir around and cook for 2-3 minutes or until pink and curled.
  6. Spoon grits into serving bowl, Top with sausage and cooked shrimp.

Makes 6 servings
