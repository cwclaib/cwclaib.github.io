Williston Clam Chowder - Seafood

Ingredients:

  • 1 qt water
  • 1 qt clams, chopped fine
  • 2 medium white potatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/4 lb salt pork, cubed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cube pork and fry until crisp and brown
  2. Cube potatoes and onions
  3. Bring clams and water to a boil
  4. Add pork, drippings, potatoes, onions, salt and pepper and cook for 1 hour; add water as needed to keep chowder from going dry
  5. Add cornmeal dumplings in the last 15 minutes of cooking if desired

Source: Mrs. Lydia Wade, Carteret County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
