Shrimp Louisiana Style - Seafood


Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp. chopped onion
  • 2/3 cup cooked or canned shrimp, broken into pieces
  • 2/3 cup hot cooked rice
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 tsp. celery salt
  • 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • few grains Cayenne
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook butter with onion for 5 minutes, storring constantly.
  2. Add shrimp, rice, and cream.
  3. When thoroughly heated, add salt, celery salt, Cayenne, and ketchup.

Serves 4 to 6

Source: Mrs. Charles D. Farmer, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
