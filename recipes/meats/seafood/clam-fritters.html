Clam Fritters - Seafood

Ingredients:

  • 24 soft clams
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup clam liquor (clam extract -- NOT broth)
  • 2 eggs
  • salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Maker a batter of flour, baking powder, salt, milk, clam liquor, and well beaten eggs
  2. Chop clams, season with salt and pepper
  3. Add to the batter and drop by tablespoonfuls into deep fat

You can tell by the recipe below that my folks were “coast people.” As far as I know the recipe is much over a hundred years old.

A very, very similar from Mrs. J. R. Mayberry, of Carteret County, includes the following details:

  • Batter should be thin like pancake batter
  • Fry on hot griddle or heavy skillet in shallow grease
  • Fritters should be 3-to-4 inches in diameter

Source: Mrs. L. D. Tomlinson, Sr., Wilson County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
