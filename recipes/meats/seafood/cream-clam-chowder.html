Cream Clam Chowder - Seafood


Ingredients:

  • 1 doz. clams, with liquid
  • 3 large white potatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 pints milk
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • flour

Instructions:

  1. Grind 1 dozen clams in meat grinder. Then, put them back in liquid from clams and heat for 15 minutes.
  2. Dice 3 large white potatoes and 1 large onion and put through grinder.
  3. Cook potatoes and onion with small amount of water for half hour.
  4. Just before serving, add 3 points milk, 1 tsbp butter and flour to thicken.
  5. Then add clams and serve immediately.

Serves 8

Source: [NOT CREDITED], Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
