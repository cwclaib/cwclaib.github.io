Vichyssoise - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 5 chicken bouillon cubes (or 10 cups stock)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 5 leeks, minced fine (or 1-1/2 cups minced green onions)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp minced chives
  • 1 tsp paprika

Instructions:

  1. Place potatoes, water, bouillon cubes, butter, and leeks in a large saucepan. Cover and cook until potatoes are very tender, about 30 minutes.
  2. Put mix into blender and blend until very smooth
  3. Pour into a saucepan and add cream, milk, salt and pepper and mix well
  4. Serve cold with garnish of chives and paprika
