Vichyssoise (2) - Soups and Stew


Ingredients:

  • 5 medium sized Irish potatoes
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • white consomme / chicken broth / water (to cover)
  • 1 pint milk
  • 1 pint single cream
  • chopped chives
  • finely cut cucumber
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Peel and slice 5 medium sized irish potatoes and 2 medium sized onions.
  2. Cook in a white consomme or chicken broth or water to cover. Let simmer 30 to 35 minutes.
  3. Strain and add 1 pint milk and 1 pint single cream. Bring to a boil.
  4. Let cool and strain. If desired, add a few chopped chives and finely cuy cucumber.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste.

If serving cold, you ma prefer to whip the heavy cream and put on soup just before serving. Serves 8.

Source: Pauline W. Koonce, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
