Gaston Beef Stew - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb salt pork
  • 2 lbs beef
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1-1/2 cloves garlic
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 bouillon cube, dissolved in 1 cup hot water
  • 8 oz canned tomato sauce
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup dry wine
  • 6 medium potatos
  • 6 carrots, quartered
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Cut into small cubes 1/2 lb salt pork and 2 lbs beef. Saute the pork in a large skillet over a slow fire. Brown the meat in the drippings over a quick fire.
  2. Sprinkle into the cooked meat and drippings flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. Separately, combine and heat until boiling the garlic, onion, bouillon, tomato sauce, cloves, parsley, and bay leaf.
  4. Place the meat in a Dutch oven. Pour the other ingredients over it. Simmer it covered for about 4 hours; after 3 hours of cooking, add 1/2 a cup of dry wine.
  5. Cook separately until nearly tender the potatos, carrots, and celery. Add these vegetables for the last 15 minutes of the meat cooking.

This is a hearty one dish meal.

Source: Mrs. A. R. Tucker, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
