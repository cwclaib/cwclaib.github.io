Popcorn Soup - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • 2-1/2 cups fresh corn
  • 2-1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1-1/2 tsp salt
  • dash of pepper
  • popcorn (popped)
  • 1/2 cup half and half

Instructions:

  1. Cook corn in 2-1/2 cups of milk until tender
  2. Melt butter and saute onion until soft; stir in flour, salt, and pepper
  3. Stir in additional milk (1 cup), half and half, and add cooked corn
  4. Sprinkle top of soup with popcorn before serving

Source: We Make You Kindly Welcome (1970)
