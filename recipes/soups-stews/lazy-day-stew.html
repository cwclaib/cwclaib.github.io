Lazy Day Stew - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef cubes (do NOT brown)
  • potatoes as desired
  • carrots as desired
  • celery as desired
  • onions as desired
  • 8 oz tomato sauce
  • 8 oz water
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tsp quick tapioca

Instructions:

  1. Spread meat in a single layer in a 9 x 13 baking dish.
  2. Peel and cut vegetables in chunks; layer on top of meat.
  3. Combine tomato sauce, water, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and salt and pepper and pour over meat and veggie mix; sprinkle dry tapioca on top. Do not stir
  4. Seal with foil, bake at 325 for 2+ hours -- do not peek!

Serves 6

(Note: may be cooked longer at a lower temperature)

Source: Foods That Rate At NC State (1983)
