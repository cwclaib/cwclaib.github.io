Dad's She-Crab Soup - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • 2 gallons milk
  • 3 lbs unsalted butter
  • 4 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1/2 celery stalk
  • 2 large green peppers
  • 2 large yellow onions
  • 1 bunch spring onion
  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Two 14-oz cans of tomato paste
  • Two 14-oz cans chopped seedless tomatoes
  • 5 lbs imitation crab meat, lightly chopped
  • 1 cup brandy
  • 1 cup worcestershire sauce
  • 1-1/2 cups lemon juice (~8 juiced lemons)
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white pepper
  • 1 cup sriracha sauce
  • 4 lb crab claw meat
  • Sherry

Instructions:

  1. Heat milk separately
  2. In a large dish, combine butter, garlic, celery, green pepper, and spring onion. Saute until soft (15 minutes)
  3. Slowly add 5 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 cup at a time, to vegetable mix and cook for 10-20 minutes
  4. Slowly add milk to roux until you reach 2 gallons
  5. Add tomatoes (paste and chopped), crab meat, brandy, worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, salt, white pepper, sriracha sauce, and crab claw meat and stir constantly for 25 minutes
  6. Add about a teaspoon of sherry per cup of soup at the time of serving
