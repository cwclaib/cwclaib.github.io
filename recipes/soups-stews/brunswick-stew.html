Brunswick Stew - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs beef
  • 2 cans (No. 2) corn (2.5 cups / 20 oz)
  • 1/2 gallon canned tomatoes
  • 4 green peppers
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 6 large onions
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 1 pint okra
  • 1 lb butter
  • 1 dozen Irish potatoes

Instructions:

  1. Cook beef tender and grind
  2. Cook remaining ground ingredients in beef broth until done
  3. Mix with beef and cook slowly over low heat 1 hour, stirring constantly

Source: Mrs. E. F. House, Sampson County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
