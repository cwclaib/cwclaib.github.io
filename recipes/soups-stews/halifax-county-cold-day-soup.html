Halifax County Cold Day Soup - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • 4 qts water
  • 1 large cracked soup bone
  • 1/2 lbs stew beef
  • 3 slices of bacon, or 3 tbsp drippings
  • 2 16-oz can tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 medium head of cabbage
  • 6 large carrots
  • 6 large onions
  • 6 stalks celery, with leaves
  • 4 medium Irish potatoes
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Instructions:

  1. Cook bacon (or heat bacon drippings) and brown beef in bacon drippings
  2. Place bone and beef in cold water, using an 8 qt pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer (Long, slow cooking is necessary)
  3. Using blender or food processor, puree the tomatoes, cabbage, carrots, onions, and celery -- if using a blender, add boiling water as needed to help puree)
    1. NOTE: In my opinion, this recipe is better if the cabbage, carrots, and celery are chopped rather than pureed
  4. Add pureed vegetables to soup pot
  5. Puree potatoes and set aside
  6. After pot has simmered for about 2 hours, remove bones and meat pieces with slotted spoon. Discard bone and chop meat into small pieces and return to pot
  7. Stir very frequently, add salt and sugar, and then simmer soup for 1 more hour
  8. Add pureed potatoes and continue to stir frequently; cook 1/2-to-1 hour on low heat (potatoes will thicken soup rapidly)

Yields 2 gallons -- freezes well

Source: Foods That Rate At NC State (1983)
