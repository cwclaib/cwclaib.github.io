Mock Turtle Soup - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • 1 shank of beef--3 to 4 lbs
  • 4 white potatoes
  • 1 small can tomatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tbsp whole cloves
  • salt to taste
  • 4 hard cooked eggs
  • 3 lemons
  • 1/2 pint sherry wine

Instructions:

  1. Cover the first 4 ingredients well with water to cook and let stand overnight
  2. Skim and strain; when ready to use, put strained juice on stove with remaining 4 ingredients in and place 3 lemons in bottom of dish
  3. Have 1/2 pint sherry wine and add some to each serving when ready

Source: Mrs. E. N. Elliott, Chowan County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
