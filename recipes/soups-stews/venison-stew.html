Venison Stew - Soups and Stews

Ingredients:

  • Shoulder or saddle of venison
  • Vinegar
  • 3 or 4 onions, sliced
  • 1/2 dozen bay leafs

Instructions:

  1. Place meat in an earthen jar and cover with vinegar
  2. To the vinegar add 3 or 4 onions, sliced, and 1/2 dozen bay leaves before pouring over the meat.
  3. Allow to stand at least two days before cooking, then remove meat from the brine and wash it in cold water
  4. Add boiling water and stew slowly until tender.
  5. Remove meat and make gravy from the broth.

Source: Foods and Nutrition Department, Home Demonstration Division of the Extension Service - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
