Brunswick Stew (Chicken) - Soups and Stews


Ingredients:

  • 1 large hen
  • 1/2 lb fat side meat, cubed
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 1/2 gal. canned tomatoes
  • 6 large potatoes, cubed
  • 2 pint cans butterbeans
  • 2 pint cans cream-style corn
  • Salt, to taste
  • Tobasco sauce, to taste
  • Worcestershire sauce, to taste
  • Prepared mustard, to taste
  • Sherry, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook 1 large hen until tender. Cut into small pieces and reserve 1 qt. or more of stock.
  2. Fry fat side meat; brown in fat chopped onions.
  3. Put cubed pork, onions, stock, and canned tomatoes in large kettle. Add potatoes and mash as they cook.
  4. When nearly done, add cans of butterbeans and cream-style corn.
  5. Add chopped chicken. Season highly with salt, Tobasco, Worcestershire sauce, prepared mustard, and sherry.

Yields 8 qts. or 24 servings

Source: Gertrude Hill, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
