French Toast - Sides and Sauces


Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 6 slices of bread*
  • salt
  • granulated sugar and cinnamon, mixed
  • fat or shortening

* do not use fresh bread unless that is all that is available. If the bread is a week or ten days old... great!

Instructions:

  1. Cut slices of bread in half.** Combine eggs, milk and flour. Dip each slice of bread quickly into the mixture, be sure both sides are covered.
  2. Brown bread in hot fat... I like about 1/2 to 1/2 inch of Crisco or Jewel shortening.
  3. While slices are cooking on second side, sprinkle with a little salt.
  4. When brown, remove from pan with a long fork or small, slotted egg turner and let drain on paper (I prefer using a paper bag rather than a paper towel).
  5. Place cooked slices on a cookie sheet, sprinkle with a little granulated sugar mixed with cinnamon.
  6. Place cinnamon-sugar covered slices under the broiler in your oven just long enough for the sugar to melt and form a glaze, watch constantly to prevent burning.
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle with powdered sugar.

You may like to serve maple syrup with this.
