Cheese Grit Casserole - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 3/4 cup quick cooking grits
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • 3 tbsp butter/margarine
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground red pepper
  • 6-8 oz cooked ham, finely diced

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. In a large saucepan, bring milk and water to a boil over medium heat
  3. Once boiling, stir in grits and bring to a boil again
  4. Once boiling, reduce heat to medium low and let simmer for 5-7 minutes (or until thick). Stir occasionally
  5. Once thickened, remove from heat and add eggs, cheese, butter, salt, and ground red pepper -- stir until well blended
  6. Lastly, add ham
  7. Pour food into a greased 1-1/2 - quart baking dish and bake for 30-40 minutes or until top is set and lightly puffed

This recipe originally called for just 4 oz of ham, sliced, but that felt much too skimpy the first time I tried it. The second time around, I did 8 oz and it was much better in my opinion.

Source: Collard Greens, Watermelons and "Miss" Charlotte's Pie (1993, 1996)
