Creamed Beans - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint navy beans
  • 1 pint tomatoes
  • 1 cup sweet cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 or 2 onions (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook navy beans.
  2. Pour off most of water and add tomatoes, cream, sugar, salt, and pepper, and optionally, onions.
  3. Set in oven to brown.

Source: Mrs. Duncan Holder, Harnett County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
