Refrigerator Pickles - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 6 lbs of 4- to-6" cucumbers, cut lengthwise into halves
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup canning salt
  • 1 quart vinegar
  • 1 quart water
  • 3 tbsp mixed spices (written: crispy crystals)
  • Green or dr dill (1 head per jar)

Instructions:

  1. Wash and drain cucumbers.
  2. Combine sugar, salt, vinegar, and water in a large saucepot.
  3. Tie spices in a spice bag; add spice bag to vinegar mixture; simmer 15 minutes.
  4. Pack cucumbers into hot jars, leaving 1/4" headspace; put one head of dill in each jar. Ladle hot liquid over cucumbers, leaving 1/2" headspace. Remove air bubbles. Adjust two-piece caps.
  5. Process pints and quarts 15 minutes in a boiling water-canner.

Source: unknown clipping from a friend's mom

Yields: about 7 pints or 3 quarts
