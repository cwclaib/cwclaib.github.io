Spaghetti Sauce - Sides and Sauces


Ingredients:

  • 2 large onions
  • 1-1/2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 cup dry wine (red or white)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1 large can tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 lb salt pork
  • 1 can mushrooms

Instructions:

  1. Brown meat, onion and garlic. Add the wine and simmer for 20 minutes covered. Add tomato paste and cook a little longer.
  2. Put mixture into a large sauce pan, add bay leaf, oregano, salt and pepper, tomato sauce and tomatoes. Simmer several hours.
  3. If sauce becomes too thick add a little boiling water with a bouillon cube. Stir in sugar last.

Source: Mrs. A. R. Tucker, Jr., Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
