Barbecue Sauce - Sides and Sauces


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 bottle ketchup
  • 1/2 bottle Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 or 4 shakes of Tabasco
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 3/4 stick butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • a little sugar, if needed

Instructions:

  1. Blend all above ingredients well

Source: Mrs. Everett Briggs, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
