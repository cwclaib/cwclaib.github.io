Wilbur's Potato Salad - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled
  • 1/2 cup salad dressing
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup salad cubes

Instructions:

  1. Boil and mash potatoes
  2. Add remaining ingredients, mix thoroughly (or blend) and chill before serving

Optional: add 2 hard boiled eggs, chopped

Source: Jim Valvano's Guide to Great Eating (1984)
