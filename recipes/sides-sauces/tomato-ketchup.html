Tomato Ketchup - Sides and Sauces


Ingredients:

  • 1 peck ripe tomatoes
  • 12 large onions
  • 3 red and 3 green hot peppers
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 6 tbsp black pepper
  • 6 tbsp celery seed
  • 6 tbsp salt
  • 6 tbsp dry mustard
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • pinch of horseradish
  • 1-1/2 pints vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Cut tomatoes, onions, and peppers and mix together. Put on stove and boil until soft. Run through food grinder.
  2. Then add remaining ingredients. Boil until as thick as desired (slowly, for about 2 hours or less).
  3. Put in sterilized jars and seal.

Source: Mrs. Everett Briggs, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
