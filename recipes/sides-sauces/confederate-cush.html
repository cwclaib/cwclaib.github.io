Confederate Cush - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • Cornbread
  • Biscuits
  • 1 cup cut up onions
  • Meat grease

Instructions:

  1. Crumble cornbread and biscuits, 1 part each, and add onions
  2. Fry meal in fried meat grease, stirring often while frying. Serve hot
  3. Optionally, water may be added. Cook until the water has been cooked out

Makes 1 quart

Source: Mrs. Jim Bell, Cabarrus County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
