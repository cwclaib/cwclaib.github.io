Glen White's Barbeque Dip - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 2 gal. vinegar
  • 1 lb red pepper
  • 1 lb black pepper
  • 1 lb horseraddish
  • 1 gal. ketchup
  • 1 large bottle mustard

Instructions:

  1. Combine vinegar, red pepper, and black pepper in a large pot and stir
  2. Add remaining ingredients, stir, and bring to boil for 5 minutes.

My barbecue sauce is vinegar based, and this recipe was around when I came along [circa 1950s].

Glen White lives in Cabarrus County, North Carolina.

Source: Holy Smoke: The Big Book of North Carolina Barbecue (2008)
