Brackett Bar-B-Q Sauce - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 tsp Tabasco
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Heat, mix, and brush on as turkey breast cooks. Takes about 1-1/2 hours

Makes 1 quart

Source: Scott Brackett, winner of the McDowell County 4-H bar-b-q contest, 1987
