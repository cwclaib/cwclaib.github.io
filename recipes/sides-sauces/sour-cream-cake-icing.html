Sour Cream Cake Icing - Desserts


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup 4X sugar
  • 5 tbsp cocoa
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • hot coffee

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter, sugar and cocoa.
  2. Add coffee to make a spreading consistency.

See also: Sour Cream Cake

Source: Mrs. William A. Howell, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
