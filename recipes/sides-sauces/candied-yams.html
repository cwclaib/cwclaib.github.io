Candied Yams - Sides and Sauces


Ingredients:

  • 6 yams, cooked and peeled
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Slice yams and arrange in a shallow casserole dish
  2. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon, dot with butter, and pour water over all.
  3. Cover and bake ina moderate oven (350) for 30 minutes.
  4. Remove cover and bake 15 minutes.

Makes 6 servings

Source: Mrs. Carl Stephens, Columbus County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
