Cocktail Sauce - Sides and Sauces


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup finely minced celery1 tsbp horseradish
  • 3 good dashes Tabasco

Instructions:

  1. Mix ingredients well.

Makes a little over 1 cup. Use on shrimp or oysters.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
