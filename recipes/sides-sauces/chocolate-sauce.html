Chocolate Sauce - Sides and Sauces


Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup shaved chocolate
  • 1/4 cup cocoa
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup thin cream
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Melt 1/4 cup butter in a sauce pan. Add 1/4 cup shaved chocolate. Keep over low flame until smooth.
  2. Add 1/4 cup smooth cocoa, 3/4 cup sugar, 1/2 cup thin cream, pinch of salt and 1 tsp vanilla.
  3. Bring to boiling point. Chill. Reheat as needed.

Source: Mrs. Belle H. Stronach, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
