Wilbur's Cole Slaw - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 1 small cabbage, ~1 lb
  • 3/4 cup salad dressing
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Remove outer leaves and core of cabbage; cut head in half and shred fine, then coarsely chop shreds
  2. Add salad dressing, sugar, mustard, and vinegar. Mix well.
  3. Chill before serving

Source: Jim Valvano's Guide to Great Eating (1984)
