Tar Heel Hush Puppies - Sides and Sauces


Ingredients:

  • 5 lbs fine corn meal
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 level tsp baking soda
  • 1 quart buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. Mix ingredients, stirring and adding water to make batter of thick consistency.
  2. Roll into oblong cakes and drop in deep, hot fat (preferably Wesson oil).
  3. Cook at 375 degrees until well browned.

This makes a large quantity of hush puppies.

Source: Florence Jones, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
