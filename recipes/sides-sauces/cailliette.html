Cailliette - Sides and Sauces


(Stuffed Cabbage or Swiss Chards)

Ingredients:

  • Large cabbage or Swiss chard leaves
  • 3 cups stale bread crumbs
  • 1 cup grated Cheddar or Swiss cheese
  • 1 cup warm sweet (whole) milk
  • 2 eggs slightly beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Dash of nutmeg
  • Chicken or beef broth

Instructions:

  1. Prepare cabbage or Swiss chard leaves by slicing off thick ribs of leaves, being careful not to break the leaves. Pour boiling water over leaves and let stand until pliable.
  2. Pour the warm milk over bread crumbs, add eggs, cheese, and seasoning. Mix well.
  3. Place one heaping tablespoon of the mixture on each leaf. Fold in sides first; then roll and fasten with toothpick or tie with string.
  4. Drop in boiling broth and boil gently for 1/2 hour or until leaf is cooked tender.

Instead of boiling, these may be placed in a covered baking dish, covered with tomato sauce and baked slowly until tender.

A substitute for the cheese would be one cup of sausage and only one egg. This should be cooked in the broth rather than baked.

Source: Mrs. John Guigou, Burke County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
