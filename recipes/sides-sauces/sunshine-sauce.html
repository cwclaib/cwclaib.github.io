Sunshine Sauce - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk, chilled
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup mild honey
  • 1 tbsp fortified margarine, melted
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp grated nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp cider vinegar (or brandy)

Instructions:

  1. Chill milk thoroughly, then whip until light and fluffy, adding lemon juice during whipping.
  2. Stir in the egg yolk; gradually beat in the mild honey and the margarine.
  3. Fold in salt, nutmeg and vinegar or brandy.

This sauce "will stand uo" several hours.

Source: "Sunshine Sauce" - The Knoxville News, Dec. 6 1946, p. 10
