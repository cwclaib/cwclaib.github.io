Rum Sauce - Sides and Sauces


For Rum Cake.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup rum
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup water together for 5 minutes.
  2. Let cool. Add 1/2 cup rum and 1/4 tsp salt.

Source: Mrs. H. M. Stilley, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
