Tar Heel Hushpuppies - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fine cornmeal
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients, adding water last to make batter a thick consistency
  2. Drop into fat and cook at 375 until golden brown

The recipe for Tar Heel Hushpuppies is the one used at the Sanitary Fish Market in Morehead City, and the recipe for which “Tony” Seamon is famous. Serves 6

Source: Mrs. J. L. Seamon, Carteret County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
