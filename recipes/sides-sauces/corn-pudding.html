Corn Pudding - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • 1 qt tender corn
  • 2 or 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • milk
  • ~4 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients except butter and mix well. Add enough milk to mix easily.
  2. Put a lump of butter, the size of an egg (4 tbsp) on top.
  3. Bake in slow to medium oven 24 to 60 minutes.

Source: Mrs. F. C. Willey, Gates County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
