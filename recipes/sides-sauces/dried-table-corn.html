Dried Table Corn - Sides and Sauces

Ingredients:

  • Corn on the cob
  • butter or meat drippings
  • seasonings to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil corn on the cob until milk will not run when a grain is broken
  2. Cut grains from cob and dry in sun; once dry, store corn in bag or bottle.
  3. Soak overnight before using.
  4. To serve, simmer and season well with butter or meat drippings

Source: Mrs. Jim R. Bell, Cabarrus County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
