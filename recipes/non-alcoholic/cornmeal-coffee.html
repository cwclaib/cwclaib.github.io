Cornmeal Coffee - Non-Alcoholic Drinks

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint well-roasted cornmeal
  • boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Add to cornmeal as much water as an ordinary sized coffeepot will hold
  2. Boil it well
