Hot Spiced Cider - Non-Alcoholic Drinks

Ingredients:

  • 1 quart cider
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • 8 short sticks cinnamon
  • 12 whole cloves
  • 8 whole allspice

Instructions:

  1. Combine ingredients and heat to boiling
  2. Cool and let stand for a few hours for flavors to blend
  3. Just before serving reheat and strain out spices
