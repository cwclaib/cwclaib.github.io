Cider Punch - Non-Alcoholic Drinks

Ingredients:

Cider/Apple Juice 2 quarts 1 gallon 2 gallons
3" sticks of cinnamon 2 4 8
Whole cloves 12 24 2 tsp
Pineapple juice 1 No. 1 can (1-1/4 cup) 1 quart 2 quarts
Orange juice 6 cups 3 quarts 6 quarts
Lemon juice 2 cups 1 quart 2 quarts
Crushed Pineapple 1 No. 2 can (2-1/2 cups) 1 No. 2-1/2 can (3-1/2 cups) 6 quarts
Ginger ale 1-1/2 quarts 3 quarts 6 quarts

It's worth noting that these ingredients don't always scale in a 1:1 ratio. Maybe it was for convenience reasons (i.e., the No. 1 sized can) or maybe it just scales oddly. This is how it appeared in the recipe book I transcribed it from.

Instructions:

  1. Simmer 2 cups of cider with spices in a saucepan about 15 minutes.
  2. Cool and remove spices; chill juices until icy cold.
  3. Combine ingredients; pour into punch bowl.
  4. Add a block of ice. Garnish with thin slices of orange and maraschino cherry halves

Source: Mrs. Dewitt Lee, Union County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
