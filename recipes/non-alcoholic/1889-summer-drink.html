1889 Summer Drink - Non-Alcoholic Drinks


Ingredients:

  • 2 oz tartaic acid
  • 2 lbs white sugar
  • 3 lemons, sliced
  • 2 quarts water
  • 4 egg whites, frothed
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 oz wintergreen

Instructions:

  1. Boil first four ingredients for 5 minutes; cool.
  2. When almost cold add egg whites, flour, and wintergreen.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of mixture to glass of water, soda may be used for effervescence.

Source: Richard Price, Mardel Corporate - Hobby Lobby Stores 21st Anniversary Cookbook (1993)
