Hot Spiced Tea - Non-Alcoholic Drinks

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup fresh ginger, thinly sliced
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 tbsp honey or 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • lemon wedge (to garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, simmer ginger, honey, cinnamon, and water for 20 minutes. Simmer longer for stronger tea.
  2. Add honey or suger and strain tea through a sieve.
