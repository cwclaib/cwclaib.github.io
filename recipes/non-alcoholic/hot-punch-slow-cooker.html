Slow Cooker Hot Punch - Non-Alcoholic Drinks

Ingredients:

  • 2 quarts apple juice
  • 3 (1 inch) cinnamon sticks
  • 1 pint, 2 oz can pineapple juice
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 46 oz can orange juice frozen concentrate
  • 15 whole cloves
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 medium orange

Instructions:

  1. Stud orange with cloves
  2. Combine juices and spices
  3. Head in slow-cooker for 3-5 hours

Source: Though I failed to note it, I'm almost certain this recipe comes from Foods That Rate at NC State (1983), however I no longer have the book to confirm it.
