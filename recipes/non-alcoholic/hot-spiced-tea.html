Hot Spiced Tea - Non-Alcoholic Drinks

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp whole cloves
  • 1 inch stick cinnamon
  • 3 quarts water
  • 2-1/2 tbsp black tea
  • juice of 3 oranges
  • juice of 1-1/2 lemons
  • 1 cup sugar

Instructions:

  1. Tie spices loosely in bag and bring to a boil in water
  2. Add tea tied loosely in bag and allow to stand 5 minutes
  3. Remove bags of spices and tea and add fruit juice and sugar

Serves 25

Source: Mrs. N. W. Maness, Harnett County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
