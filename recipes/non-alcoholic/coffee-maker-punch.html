Coffee Maker Punch - Non-Alcoholic Drinks

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups apple juice
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • dash of salt
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • 1-1/2 tsp whole cloves
  • 1-1/2 tsp allspice

Instructions:

  1. In bottom of percolator, combine apple juice, brown sugar, and salt
  2. In basket place cinnamon sticks, cloves, and allspice
  3. Percolate as you would coffee

Source: Though I failed to note it, I'm almost certain this recipe comes from Foods That Rate at NC State (1983), however I no longer have the book to confirm it.
