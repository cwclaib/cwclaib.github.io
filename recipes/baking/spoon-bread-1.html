Spoon Bread (1) - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup meal
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp butter or lard
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1-1/2 cups boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Pour meal, salt and lard into bowl. Add boiling water and beat until smooth.
  2. Break egg into mixture, then add milk into which baking powder has been dissolved and mix well.
  3. Pour into buttered baking dish and cook in 400 degree oven for 30 minutes.

Source: Mrs. Sprague Silver, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
