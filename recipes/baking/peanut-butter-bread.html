Peanut Butter Bread - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Sift dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, salt) together
  2. Work peanut butter in with a fork, add milk to make dough soft
  3. Pour into greased loaf pan and bake 50-60 minutes in a moderate oven (350-375)

Source: I failed to note where exactly this recipe came from, however it was from somewhere in the McDowell County Extension Homemakers papers; I don't believe it was the same Holiday House Cookbook that many of the other recipes came from.
