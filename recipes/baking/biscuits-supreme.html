Biscuits Supreme - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour, sifted
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup skim milk

Instructions:

  1. Sift flour, salt, baking powder, cream of tartar, and sugar together 3 times
  2. Cut in shortening with fork until mixture resembles coarse crumbs
  3. Add milk all at once and stir just until dough follows fork around bowl
  4. Pat or roll 1/2‚Äù thick, then cut biscuits with biscuit cutter
  5. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 450 for 10-12 minutes

Yields 16 medium biscuits
