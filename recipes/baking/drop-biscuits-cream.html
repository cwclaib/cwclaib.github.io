Drop Biscuits (Cream) - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 cups chilled heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a large bowl
  3. Add cream and then stir just until the dough firms up
  4. Drop large 1/4 cups of batter about 1 inch apart on an ungreased large baking sheet. Bake in the middle of the oven until the tops are pale and golden and the bottoms are golden brown, about 18-20 minutes
