Macaroni and Cheese - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups macaroni
  • 1/2 lb cheese
  • 1 cup soda crackers, crushed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pint sweet milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook macaroni in water until tender.
  2. Place layer of macaroni, cheese, and cracker crumbs in baking dish, continuing until all is used.
  3. Thoroughly beat eggs, add milk, then pour over macaroni and sprinkle with pepper and salt
  4. Dot with butter, bake in moderate oven (350) for 30 minutes.

Source: Mrs. W. J. Cotton, Harnett County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
