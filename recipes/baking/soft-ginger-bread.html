Mrs. C. R. Lee's Soft Ginger Bread - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup molasses
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1/2 cup melted shortening
  • 1 tsp cloves
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking soda (dissolved in water)

Instructions:

  1. Combine shortening and molasses.
  2. Add sifted dry ingredients alternately with hot water, beating until smooth.
  3. Bake in a greased 6 x 10 inch pan in moderate oven (350 degrees) for 45 minutes.
  4. Serve warm.

Source: Mrs. Robert M. Buckett, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
