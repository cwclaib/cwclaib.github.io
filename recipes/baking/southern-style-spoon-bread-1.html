Southern Style Spoon Bread (1) - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cornmeal, unsifted
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 3 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients and butter thoroughly
  2. Add water gradually until a thick pasty dough has been formed, then add stiffly beaten eggs
  3. Bake in slow (375 F) oven until done.
  4. Serve hot with butter
