Corn Muffins - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup meal
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup sweet cream or enough to make batter

Note: no instructions provided. Assumed:

  1. Combine Ingredients
  2. Pour into muffin tin, bake at 400 for 15 minutes

This is a recipe that has been handed down for many years in the Chatham family. The recipe was given to Mrs. Ketchie by her mother, the former Ida Chatham of Elkin. It has been a favorite within the family for many years. This makes six muffins.

Source: Mrs. Frank Ketchie, Iredell County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
