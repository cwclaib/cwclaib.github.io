Mrs. Snypes' Pie Crust - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Red Band flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup Crisco
  • 4 tbsp ice water

Instructions:

  1. Mix salt with flour. Cut in butter and crisco until shortening is in pieces not larger than peas.
  2. Pour into ice water and stir with fork until mixture is dampened.
  3. Roll in ball, wrap in waxed paper, and chill thoroughly.

If this crust is baked slower and longer than usual, it will be more satisfactory for a pie that is to be refrigerated for any length of time.

Source: Mrs. William B. Grimes, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
