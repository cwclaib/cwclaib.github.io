Scotch Short Bread - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb butter
  • 2-3/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter; add sugar slowly, then flour, 2 tbsp at a time.
  2. Knead until smooth. Pat out a small amount at a tine and cut with a small cookie cutter, or shape into roll or block, chill, and slice off.
  3. Bake at 400 degrees until a very delicate brown. Do not overcook.

This short bread is very much like commercial Lorna Doones. It is rich and so good it melts in your mouth.

Source: Ruth Firth, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
