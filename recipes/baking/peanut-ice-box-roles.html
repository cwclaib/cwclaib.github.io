Peanut Ice-Box Rolls - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 yeast cake (3 packages dry yeast)
  • 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 cup soft lard
  • 1-1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups sifted flour
  • 1 egg, unbeaten
  • 1 cup finely ground peanuts

Instructions:

  1. Soften yeast cake in lukewarm water
  2. Mix lard and sugar together in mixing bowl; add boiling water to mix and stir well.
  3. Stir in softened yeast, then add salt to flour. Stir in half of flour and beat well with spoon.
  4. Add egg and beat to mix well. Add rest of flour and mix.
  5. Cover bowl with waxed paper and put right in refrigerator.
  6. After some time, remove dough from refrigerator and fold in freshly parched, ground peanuts.
  7. Let rise until double in bulk.
  8. Punch down, mold into any desired shape.
  9. Place in moderately greased pans and let rise until double in bulk.
  10. Bake in a moderately hot oven (425 F) 15-to-20 minutes.

Yield 1-1/2 dozen rolls (18)

Source: Mrs. Fred L. Johnson, Northampton County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
