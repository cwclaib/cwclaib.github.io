Sour Milk Biscuits - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/3 cup lard or shortening
  • 3/4 cup sour milk (or 1 cup fresh milk + 1 tbsp lemon juice/vinegar + wait 5 min)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, salt and soda.
  2. Cut in lard or shortening til mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Stir in milk just til dry ingredients are moistened
  4. Knead on lightly floured surface 10-12 times
  5. Roll out or pat to 1/2” thickness, then cut into 2-1/2” rounds
  6. Place on ungreased baking sheet and bake at 450 for 10-12 minutes

Serves 12

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
