Angel Flake Biscuits - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 package dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp warm water
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 cups sifted flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cup shortening

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast in water. Mix yeast mixture with buttermilk, sugar, and salt. Set aside.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder and baking soda together. Cut in shortening.
  3. Add liquid mixture and mix lightly. DO NOT KNEAD. Refrigerate overnight.
  4. Pinch off amounts as needed; knead, roll, and cut. DO NOT ALLOW TO RISE.
  5. Bake at 450 for 10 minutes.

Source: Foods That Rate at NC State (1983)
