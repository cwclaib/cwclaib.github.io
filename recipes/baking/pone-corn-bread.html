Pone Corn Bread - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 quart cornmeal
  • 1 pinch baking soda (1/8 tsp)
  • salt measured in palm of hand (1 tsp)
  • Buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. Mix with buttermilk until soft enough to shape into pones
  2. Brown quickly on griddle then place in medium hot oven (375) and bake 30 minutes

This 100 year old recipe was given to Mrs. Willey by Mrs. Pearl Lyons. She used to cook her pones in a covered spider [pan] in fireplace with hot coals on top until good and brown.

Source: Mrs. F. C. Wiley, Gates County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
