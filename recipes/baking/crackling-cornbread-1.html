Crackling Cornbread (1) - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup crackling, diced
  • 1 cup sour milk
  • 2 cups corn meal
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix and sift together dry ingredients, except crackling
  2. Add milk, then stir in the cracklings
  3. Form into oblong cakes and place in greased baking pan
  4. Bake in a hot oven (400 F) for 30 minutes.

Source: Mrs. N. V. Daniel, Granville County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
