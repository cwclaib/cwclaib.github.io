Drop Biscuits (Milk/Buttermilk) - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1-1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (8 tbsp, 1 stick) -- cold
  • 3/4 - to - 1 cup of milk or buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Optionally, add herbs.
  3. Cut the cold butter into small pieces and cut into the chunks with a fork or pastry cutter until the mix reaches a coarse consistency. You can add cheeses here before adding milk
  4. Add milk and stir until just combined. If the batter is dry, you can add more. DO NOT OVERMIX -- the batter will be quite lumpy
  5. Drop large mounds of the mix on a baking sheet (about 1/4 cup each)
  6. Bake at 450 for 18-22 minutes or until golden brown
  7. Brush with melted butter

Source: Though I failed to note it, I'm almost certain this recipe comes from Foods That Rate at NC State (1983), however I no longer have the book to confirm it.
