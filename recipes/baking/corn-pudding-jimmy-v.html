Jim Valvano's Corn Pudding - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 can cream-style corn
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp margarine

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Mix ingredients together and bake for 35 minutes or until firm

Source: Jim Valvano's Guide to Great Eating (1984)
