Hot Corn Cakes - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cornmeal
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted shortening

Instructions:

  1. Scald cornmeal with boiling water
  2. Mix ingredients and cook on hot griddle. Add 1 tsp fat to the griddle before each batch of cakes

Source: Odessa Wasili, Currituck County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
