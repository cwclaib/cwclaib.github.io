Refrigerator Rolls - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • 1 yeast cake (or 1 pkg. dry yeast)
  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • 1 cup scalded milk
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mashed potatoes (roughly 2 whole potatoes)
  • 6 to 8 cups flour

Instructions:

  1. Mash potatoes. Add shortening, sugar, and salt while hot. Add eggs and cream, beating well.
  2. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water, then add to lukewarm (scalded) milk. Finally add to potato mixture.
  3. Put in enough sifted flour to make a stiff dough.
  4. Toss dough on floured board and knead well.
  5. Put in large bowl, cover, and let rise until double in bulk.
  6. Knead slightly, rub with melted shortening, put in covered container, and store in refrigerator.
  7. About 1 hour before baking time, punch off required amount and shape into rolls. Let rise until light.
  8. Bake at 400 degrees about 15 minutes.

This recipe makes between 70 and 80 party-size rolls.

Source: Gertrude Hill, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
