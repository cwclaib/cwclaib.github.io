Buttermilk Biscuits - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups self-rising flour
  • 2/3 cup buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese, OR
  • 1 tbsp herbs (parsley, rosemary, sage, thyme)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 475
  2. Pour oil and buttermilk into a cup but DO NOT STIR
  3. Add flour and stir with a fork until mixture forms a ball
  4. Press out to 1/2‚Äù thickness, cut into rounds, and bake 5 minutes; brown under broiler

Yields 16 medium biscuits
