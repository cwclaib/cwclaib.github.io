Lye Hominy - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon of corn, unshucked
  • 4-5 cups wood ashes (or 2 oz concentrated lye in 1 gal boiling water)

Instructions:

  1. Take about a gallon of corn and four-to-five cups of wood ashes and boil until hulls and eyes will slip off.
  2. Wash in cold water until eyes, hulls, and ashes are removed.
  3. Boil until tender, wash again and store until ready for use
  4. Cook corn in butter or meat drippings

This was made by slave cooks and served with butter or meat drippings

Source: Mrs. Jim R. Bell, Cabarrus County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
