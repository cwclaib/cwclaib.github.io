Cornbread - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg, well beaten
  • 2 tbsp melted shortening (butter, bacon fat, chicken fat, or beef drippings)

Instructions:

  1. Sift together cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add milk, egg, and shortening to dry ingredients.
  3. Bake in buttered 8x8 pan at 425 for 20 minutes

Serves 8

Source: Foods That Rate at NC State (1983)
