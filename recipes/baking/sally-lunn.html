Sally Lunn - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 1 heaping quart flour
  • 1/2 cake yeast (1/2 pkg. yeast)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp lard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs and sugar together.
  2. Heat milk; dissolve yeast cake in a little of the warm milk. When lukewarm, add to eggs and sugar. Then add lard and allow to cool.
  3. Add flour, mixed with salt, and milk alternately to egg and sugar mixture
  4. Let rise about 3-1/2 hours. Beat down and put into greased muffin rings or angel food cake pan.
  5. Allow to rise two more hours and bake in 450 degree oven.

Source: Mrs. Sprague Silver, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
