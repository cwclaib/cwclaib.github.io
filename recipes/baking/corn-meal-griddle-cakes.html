Corn-Meal Griddle Cakes - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup corn meal
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1-1/2 cups milk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs, well beaten

Instructions:

  1. Put meal, sugar, and salt into mixing bowl and pour over them the boiling water.
  2. Let stand until the meal swells; then add the cold milk.
  3. When the mixture is quite cool, stir in the flour and baking powder, mixing well, and lastly add the eggs, well beaten.
  4. Bake on a hot griddle. The cakes should be small, well browned, and thoroughly cooked; they need a little longer cooking than wheat griddle cakes.

Source: Mrs. Jack Wardlaw, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
