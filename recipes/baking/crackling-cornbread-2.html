Crackling Cornbread (2) - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup crackling
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 4 cups corn meal
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1-1/2 cups buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. Break up 1 cup of crackling, place them into 2 cups of hot water
  2. Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
  3. Bake slowly until done

Source: Mrs. Annie Kate Trawick, Stokes County - From North Carolina Kitchens: Favorite Recipes Old and New (1957)
