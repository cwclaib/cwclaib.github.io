Old Virginia Batter Bread - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 1 quart sweet milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 7 tbsp meal
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs and stir in meal*, flour, and salt.
  2. Bring milk to boil and add to egg mixture gradually.
  3. Cook in sauce pan until it thickens a little, stirring constantly.
  4. Pour into hot casserole dish that has the butter melted in it. Bake at 350 degrees until brown.

* This is a correction from an assumed typo in the original recipe; the original recipe said to "Beat eggs and stir in milk, flour, and salt. Bring milk to boil and add to egg mixture gradually."

Source: Mrs. Charles M. Gilliam, Jr., Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
