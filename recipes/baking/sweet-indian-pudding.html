Sweet Indian Pudding - Baking

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Coors beer
  • 1/3 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2 tbsp butter/margarine
  • 1/3 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1-1/2 cups milk

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine Coors, cornmeal, and butter. Cook and stir til boiling
  2. Reduce heat; cover and cook over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in molasses, sugar, ginger, cinnamon, and salt and mix well.
  4. Combine eggs and milk; stir into cornmeal mixture
  5. Turn into ungreased 1-qt. Casserole and bake, uncovered, at 325 in oven for 60 minutes or til a knife inserted just off-center comes out clean
  6. Serve warm or chilled (mixture may separate)

Serves 6

Source: A Taste of the West from Coors (1981)
