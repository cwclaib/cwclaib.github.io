Banana Bread - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 bananas, mashed
  • 1-1/2 cups sifted flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup nuts

Instructions:

  1. Beat egg well. Add sugar and melted butter
  2. Sift flour, soda, and salt into mixture.
  3. Add mashed banana and nuts.
  4. Bake in loaf pan at 375 degrees45 to 50 minutes.

Source: Mrs. W. W. Williams, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
