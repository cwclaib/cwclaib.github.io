Alice's Waffles - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 6 tbps melted butter
  • 3 level tsp baking powder
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 scant tsp salt
  • 2 eggs, beaten separately
  • 1-1/2 cups milk

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients well except for baking powder and egg whites.
  2. Beat egg whites with baking powder and fold in just before cooking.
  3. Pour into heated waffle irons and butter while hot.

Source: Florence Jones, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
