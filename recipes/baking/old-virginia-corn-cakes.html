Old Virginia Corn Cakes - Baking


Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 pint milk
  • 1 lb corn meal
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup melted lard

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs well and add milk.
  2. Sift meal and mix with salt, baking powder, and melted lard. Then mix with eggs and milk.
  3. Cook on a hot griddle with bacon fat.

This amount serves 6.

Source: Mrs. Robert Duckett, Christ Church Cook Book (c. 1952)
