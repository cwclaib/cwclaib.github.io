Sweet Potato Casserole - Baking

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
    • 3 cups sweet potatoes, cooked and mashed (~6 potatoes)
    • 1 stick butter or margarine (1/2 if desired)
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 tsp vanilla
    • 1 cup sugar (reduce if desired)
  • Streusel topping
    • 1/3 stick butter or margarine
    • 1 cup light brown sugar
    • 2 tbsp flour
    • 1 cup pecans, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Mix first group of ingredients together with mixer, pour into greased baking dish
  3. Mix streusel topping and sprinkle over potato mixture.
  4. Bake 30-40 minutes

Source: Collard Greens, Watermelons and "Miss" Charlotte's Pie (1993, 1996)
