Maple Muffins - Baking

Ingredients:

  • Muffins:
    • 2 eggs or 4 egg whites
    • 1 cup sour cream
    • 1 cup maple syrup
    • 1 cup flour
    • 1 cup bran flakes
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 3/4 cup hazelnuts or pecans coarsely chopped
  • Glaze:
    • 1 cup maple syrup
    • 6 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Muffins
    1. Preheat oven to 400 and grease 18 muffin tins
    2. Beat egg or egg whites with fork in large bowl to blend; stir in sour cream and maple syrup.
    3. Add flour, bran flakes, and baking soda; stir just until batter is moistened, then mix in nuts
    4. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full and bake for 15 minutes
  2. Glaze
    1. Combine syrup and butter in small saucepan over medium heat; stir until mixture is well-blended
    2. Remove muffins from pan and place 1 tsp glaze in bottom of each muffin cup
    3. Dip top of each muffin into glaze, then return to muffin cups, right side up.
    4. Let stand for about 15 minutes so bottoms absorb glaze
    5. Serve warm

Yields 18 servings

Source: Foods That Rate at NC State (1983)
