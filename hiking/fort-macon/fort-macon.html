Fort Macon State Park

I explored this area on Sunday, March 13th, 2022 with my girlfriend.

Fort Macon State Park was just the second commisioned state park in North Carolina (following Mount Mitchell), and the first to open. Since its initial construction in the 1830s, it's seen service in 3 wars: the Civil War, the Spanish-American War, and World War II.
