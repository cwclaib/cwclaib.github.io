Morrow Mountain State Park - Short Loop

I did this hike on September 19th, 2020 with a group of several friends. Unfortunately, we were forced back after fairly little hiking due to class commitments and a late start. This loop was a quick and easy 3/4-mile loop.

Note that officially this trail is referred to as "Mountain Loop Trail" and should not be confused with "Bridle: Short Loop."
