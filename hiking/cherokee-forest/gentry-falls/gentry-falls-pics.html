Backbone Rock Trail - Cherokee National Forest

I did this trail on July 25th, 2021 with one other person.

This was a very short, very easy hike The first few pictures are a view of the rock from roughly the Backbone Falls parking area. Otherwise, shots are of the short, <1/4 mile trail. Find the sign labeled Trail 53: Backbone Rock and follow the fairly clear trail path.
