Sam Branch Falls - Middle Prong Wilderness Area

I hiked this short trail on May 4th, 2023, with my mother and brother.

The hike in is fairly short and easy aside from a steep scramble from the tiny roadside parking area. The trail ends at Lower Sam Branch Falls





You can follow a small side falls upstream to Wash Hollow Falls.
