Triple Falls - DuPont State Forest

I have hiked this trail twice; the first time with my parents and brother on August 12th, 2018, and the second time on October 31st, 2020 with a group of friends.

The first time I did this trail, we followed the side of the Little River up towards the falls, which allowed us to get some nice, close-up pictures of the individual segments of Triple Falls. We did this hike after visiting Hooker Falls but before visiting High Falls or Bridal Veil Falls.











The second time I did this hike, we came via the eastern side of High Falls Loop Trail, which was at the time technically closed for trail maintenance (in our defense, we didn't see that sign until after we finished the hike). We came in the Fall which allowed for a much clearer long-distance shot of the fall.
