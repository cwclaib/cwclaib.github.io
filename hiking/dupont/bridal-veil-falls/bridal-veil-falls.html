Bridal Veil Falls - DuPont State Forest

Visited on Sunday, August 12th, 2018 with my parents and my brother.

This beautiful waterfall -- created by the Little River flowing down Cedar Rock Mountain -- is on DuPont State Forest's land, but is located fairly far away from most of the park's other most popular waterfalls, making this location a little more private and secluded.

There are several different ways you could get to Bridal Veil Falls; my family crossed the Covered Bridge at the top of High Falls after seeing Hooker Falls and Triple Falls. Heading eastward, we hiked for about a half a mile before taking a right on Conservation Road, a fairly wide gravel road, and hiked along that road for 1-3/4 miles.

I didn't take any pictures of the trail itself, but I did take several pictures of the magnificent falls.
