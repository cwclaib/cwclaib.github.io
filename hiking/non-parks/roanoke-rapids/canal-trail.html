Roanoke Rapids Canal Trail

I hiked a small portion of this trail on January 23rd, 2022 with my brother, my girlfriend, and her sister. We only hiked about 1 mile roundtrip, from the museum (which was closed for the day) to the ruins of an old hydroelectric bulkhead. We caught the start of a pretty sunset through the powerlines on the way back. There was also an adorable snowman someone else had made waiting for us at the bench of the bulkhead overlook.
