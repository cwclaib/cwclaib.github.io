Zebulon Vance Birthplace

I visited this site on October 16th, 2021 with my girlfriend.

I organized a trip in October 2021 to drive the unpaved portion of NC-197. While up there, I did a few hikes around the Blue Ridge Parkway (Rattlesnake Lodge, Craggy Gardens, and a very foggy Mount Mitchell, in addition to several scenic overlooks) and swung by former Governor Zebulon Vance's birthplace. Unfortunately, we moved slower than expected thanks to a frequent pivoting of plans caused by the rain and fog. We arrived just minutes before closing, but soon enough to speed-run the museum and the outdoor portions of the site as the buildings were getting shuttered.
