Medoc Mountain State Park - Bluff Loop

I did this hike on January 29th, 2023 with my girlfriend.

The trail is a fairly easy 3-mile loop, which starts near the playground and event area on the premises.




The one downside to this trail is that a great deal of it follows along the road which bounds the park, which reduces the scerenity of the area. Once the trail peels away from the road, you'll be confronted with Fishing Creek, which eventually takes you to the titular bluff.
