Eagle Rock Trail - Chimney Rock State Park

I did this trail on January 2nd, 2023 with my family and my girlfriend. Be aware that the parking area is down a narrow and fairly rough gravel road, and has limited parking spaces; plan accordingly.

This trail starts out somewhat steep, following a fire access road towards Eagle Rock. After about half a mile, you'll reach the rocks. Be sure to watch your step on the rocks and heed the posted signs -- there are plenty of ways to get yourself hurt on them.



If you can safely climb the rock formations, they offer excellent views of the surrounding valleys.







Once you leave the bouldering area, you begin a somewhat steep descent towards The Tunnel, an interesting rock formation below the cliffs of Eagle Rock. Be aware that, at the time of my visit, a nearby landowner let his three large dogs roam his property off-leash; the dogs would frequently come bark and investigate the passerby. They never were very aggressive, but it's certainly something to be aware of.
