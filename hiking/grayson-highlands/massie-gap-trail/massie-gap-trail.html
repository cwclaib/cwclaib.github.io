Grayson Highlands - Massie Gap Trail

I did this hike on October 3rd, 2020 with a group of 4 other friends. I believe this hike was started at Massie Gap Trail and then turned on Wilburn Ridge, however, we just sort of hiked towards Mount Rogers with the goal of getting a good view before the end of the day. I don't really have any notes about hiking this trail; it's fairly straightforward.
