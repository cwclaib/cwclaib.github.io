The Channels (Virginia)

I did this hike on Thursday, May 12th, 2022 with my brother. Because it was around noon on a weekday, we didn't see many other people -- maybe 3 other groups.

A lot of the hike is some fairly unremarkable gravel road, though occasionally some vistas do break through.


After a few miles of hiking, you reach the top of the mountain and the abandoned fire watch tower. Near there is also a little preview to the channels to come.




After walking under the fire tower, you have basically reached the Channels, which are a really unique and interesting rock formation. They're basically slot canyons in the middle of the mountains.
