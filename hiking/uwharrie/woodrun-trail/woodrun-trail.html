Woodrun Trail - Uwharrie National Forest

I hiked the Woodrun Trail on January 29th, 2022 with my girlfriend. The experience was made interesting because of a somewhat unexpected overnight snowfall which totaled about 2" in some areas. Since neither of us had ever done much snow hiking, the opportunity was an interesting one. We followed Woodrun Road (the gravel hunt camp access road) for the start of the hike, but took the foot trail on the way back.
