Pisgah Covered Bridge - Pisgah Covered Bridge and Natural Area

* Though not technically in the Uwharries, I have listed the covered bridge here because of its relative proximity.

I visited the Pisgah Covered Bridge on Saturday, January 8th, 2022, around 8 o'clock in the morning. There is a very short loop around the bridge that serves as a way to get a fuller view of the bridge.
